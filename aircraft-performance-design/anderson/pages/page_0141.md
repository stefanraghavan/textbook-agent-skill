Substituting Eq. (2.35) into (2.36), we have

$$
C_{D,0}=\frac{q_\infty S_{wet} C_{fe}}{q_\infty S}=\frac{S_{wet}}{S}C_{fe}

$$

Equation (2.37) can be used to obtain an estimate for $C_{D,0}$ by finding $S_{wet}/S$ from Fig. 2.54 and $C_{fe}$ from Fig. 2.55.

### Example 2.15

Estimate the zero-lift drag coefficient of the Boeing 747.

**Solution**

From Fig. 2.54, for the Boeing 747

$$
S_{wet}/S=6.3
$$

From Fig. 2.55, given the assumption that the Boeing 747 and the Lockheed C-5 are comparable airplanes in size and flight conditions,

$$
C_{fe}=0.0027
$$

Hence, from Eq. (2.37),

$$
C_{D,0}=\frac{S_{wet}}{S}C_{fe}=(6.3)(0.0027)=0.017
$$
