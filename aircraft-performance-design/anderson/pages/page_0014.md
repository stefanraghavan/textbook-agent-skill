# PART 1

# PRELIMINARY CONSIDERATIONS

Part 1 consists of three chapters which set the stage for our subsequent discussion of airplane performance and design. Chapter 1 is a short history of the evolution of the airplane and its design; its purpose is to set the proper philosophical perspective for the material in this book. Chapters 2 and 3 cover aspects of applied aerodynamics and propulsion, respectively, insofar as they *directly relate* to the performance and design considerations to be discussed in the remainder of this book.
