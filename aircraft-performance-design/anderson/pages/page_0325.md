Consider a jet-propelled airplane. Range is again determined by the pounds of fuel consumed per mile covered over the ground. Hence, analogous to Eq. (5.175), we write

$$
\frac{\text{lb of fuel consumed}}{\text{mi}}=\frac{(\text{TSFC})T_R}{V_g}
\qquad [5.181]
$$
