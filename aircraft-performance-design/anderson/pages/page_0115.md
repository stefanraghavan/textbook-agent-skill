## Example 2.13

Using Eq. (2.25), calculate the low-speed lift coefficient of a delta wing of aspect ratio 1.46 at an angle of attack of $20^\circ$. This is the same delta wing for which the experimental data in Fig. 2.34 apply. Compare the calculated result with the data shown in Fig. 2.34.

### Solution

From the geometry of the triangular planform shown in Fig. 2.35, the planform area $S$ is given by

$$
S = \frac{1}{2}(2s)l = sl
$$

The value of $s/l$ is determined by the aspect ratio as follows.

$$
\mathrm{AR} = \frac{b^2}{S} = \frac{(2s)^2}{sl} = \frac{4s^2}{sl} = 4\frac{s}{l}
$$
