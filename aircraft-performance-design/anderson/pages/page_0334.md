## 6.1 INTRODUCTION

Our study of static performance (no acceleration) in Chapter 5 answered a number of questions about the capabilities of a given airplane—how fast it can fly, how far it can go, etc. However, there are more questions to be asked: How fast can it turn? How high can it “zoom”? What ground distances are covered during takeoff and landing? The answers to these questions involve *accelerated* flight, the subject of this chapter. To this end, we return to the general equations of motion derived in Chapter 4, which you should review before going further.
