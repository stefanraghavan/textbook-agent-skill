$$
\frac{T_0}{T_\infty}=1+\frac{\gamma-1}{2}M_\infty^2
$$

where $T_\infty$ is the ambient static temperature and $\gamma$ is the ratio of specific heats $\gamma = c_p/c_v$. For air below a temperature of $1500^\circ R$, $\gamma = 1.4$. At $M_\infty = 3.3$, we have

$$
\frac{T_0}{T_\infty}=1+0.2(3.3)^2=3.18
$$
