## Example 5.4

For the Gulfstream IV at the conditions given in Example 5.1, calculate the maximum values of $C_L^{3/2}/C_D$, $C_L/C_D$, and $C_L^{1/2}/C_D$, as well as the flight velocities at which they occur.

### Solution

The maximum values of $C_L^{3/2}/C_D$, $C_L/C_D$, and $C_L^{1/2}/C_D$ depend only on the drag polar, where $C_{D,0} = 0.015$ and $K = 0.08$. From Eq. (5.38),

$$
\left(\frac{C_L^{3/2}}{C_D}\right)_{\max}
=
\frac{1}{4}
\left(
\frac{3}{K\,C_{D,0}^{1/3}}
\right)^{3/4}
=
\frac{1}{4}
\left[
\frac{3}{(0.08)(0.015)^{1/3}}
\right]^{3/4}
=
10.83
$$
