## 6.9 SUMMARY

This chapter has dealt with some special cases of *accelerated* airplane performance, that is, cases where the acceleration of the airplane is not zero. In some of these cases, the load factor $n$, defined as

$$
n = \frac{L}{W}
\qquad \text{[6.5]}
$$

plays an important role.

The turn radius and turn rate in a level turn are given, respectively, by

$$
R = \frac{V_{\infty}^2}{g\sqrt{n^2 - 1}}
\qquad \text{[6.9]}
$$

and

$$
\omega = \frac{g\sqrt{n^2 - 1}}{V_{\infty}}
\qquad \text{[6.11]}
$$
