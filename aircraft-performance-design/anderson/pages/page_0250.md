Since $L = W$ for steady, level flight,

$$
L = W = \frac{1}{2}\rho_\infty V_\infty^2 S C_L
\qquad\qquad [5.54]
$$

Solving Eq. (5.54) for $V_\infty$, we have

$$
V_\infty = \sqrt{\frac{2W}{\rho_\infty S C_L}}
\qquad\qquad [5.55]
$$

Substituting Eq. (5.55) into (5.53), we obtain

$$
P_R = \frac{W}{C_L/C_D}\sqrt{\frac{2W}{\rho_\infty S C_L}}
$$

or

$$
P_R = \sqrt{\frac{2W^3 C_D^2}{\rho_\infty S C_L^3}}
\qquad\qquad [5.56]
$$
