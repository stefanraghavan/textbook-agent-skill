Using the graphical approach illustrated in Fig. 5.19, obtain the maximum velocity at 30,000 ft for the Gulfstream IV described in Example 5.1.

## Solution

The power required curve is identical to that calculated in Example 5.7; it is plotted again in Fig. 5.20, extended to higher velocities. The maximum power available curve is obtained from Eqs. (5.58) and (5.61) as

$$
P_A = T_A V_\infty = (T_A)_0 \left[\frac{\rho}{\rho_0}\right]^{0.6} V_\infty = 27,700 \left[\frac{8.9068 \times 10^{-4}}{0.002377}\right]^{0.6} V_\infty = 15,371V_\infty
$$
