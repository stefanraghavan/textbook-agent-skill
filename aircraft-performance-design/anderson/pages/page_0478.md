**TRANSCONTINENTAL & WESTERN AIR INC.**

10 RICHARDS ROAD

MUNICIPAL AIRPORT

KANSAS CITY. MISSOURI

August 2nd,  
19 32

Douglas Aircraft Corporation,  
Clover Field,  
Santa Monica, California.

Attention: **Mr. Donald Douglas**

Dear Mr. Douglas:

Transcontinental & Western Air is interested  
in purchasing ten or more trimotored transport planes.  
I am attaching our general performance specifications,  
covering this equipment and would appreciate your advising  
whether your Company is interested in this manufacturing  
job.

If so, approximately how long would it take  
to turn out the first plane for service tests?

Very truly yours,

Jack Frye

Jack Frye  
Vice President  
In Charge of Operations

JF/GS  
Encl.

N.B. Please consider this information confidential and  
return specifications if you are not interested.

SAVE TIME — USE THE AIR MAIL
