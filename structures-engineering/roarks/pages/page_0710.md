Uniform pressure $q$ over length $L$ across edge of semi-infinite plate**  ![Figure 14.1: Uniform pressure over length L across edge of semi-infinite plate](figures/fig14_1.png) <!-- Description: Semi-infinite plate with a uniformly distributed load q over length L near the edge; points A, O1, O2, Q, distances d, x1, x2, r, r1, and angle α are indicated. --> | At any point $O_1$ outside loaded area,  $$y=\frac{2q}{\pi E}\left[(L+x_1)\ln\frac{d}{L+x_1}-x_1\ln\frac{d}{x_1}\right]+qL\frac{1-\nu}{\pi E}$$  At any point $O_2$ inside loaded area,  $$y=\frac{2q}{\pi E}\left[(L-x_2)\ln\frac{d}{L-x_2}+x_2\ln\frac{d}{x_2}\right]+qL\frac{1-\nu}{\pi E}$$  where $y=$ deflection relative to a remote point $A$ a distance $d$ from edge of loaded area  At any point $Q$,  $$\sigma_c=0.318q(\alpha+\sin\alpha)$$  $$\tau=0.318q\sin\alpha$$  (Ref. 6) |

Rigid cylindrical die of radius $R$ on surface of semi-infinite body; total load $P$**  ![Figure 14.2: Rigid cylindrical die on semi-infinite body](figures/fig14_2.png) <!-- Description: Rigid cylindrical die pressing downward with load P on the surface of a semi-infinite body; contact region and radius R are shown with point Q and radial coordinate r. --> | $$y=\frac{P(1-\nu^2)}{2RE}$$  At any point $Q$ on surface of contact  $$\sigma_c=\frac{P}{2\pi R\sqrt{R^2-r^2}}$$  $$(\sigma_c)_{\max}=\infty \qquad \text{at edge (theoretically)}$$  $$(\sigma_c)_{\min}=\frac{P}{2\pi R^2} \qquad \text{at center}$$  (Ref. 6) |
