## 3c. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge simply supported

![Figure 11.2-3c: Outer edge simply supported, inner edge simply supported](figures/fig11_2_3c.png)
<!-- Description: Two schematic cross-sectional diagrams of a flat circular plate with linearly increasing load q from b to a, showing inner radius r_o and simply supported outer and inner edges. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad M_{rb} = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0$

$$
\theta_b=\frac{-qa^3\left(C_3L_{18}-C_9L_{12}\right)}{D\left(C_1C_9-C_3C_7\right)}
$$

$$
Q_b=qa\frac{C_1L_{18}-C_7L_{12}}{C_1C_9-C_3C_7}
$$

$$
\theta_a=\theta_bC_4+Q_b\frac{a^2}{D}C_6-\frac{qa^3}{D}L_{15}
$$

$$
Q_a=Q_b\frac{b}{a}-\frac{q}{6a}\left(2a^2-r_oa-r_o^2\right)
$$

If $r_o=b$ (linearly increasing load from $b$ to $a$),

## 3d. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge fixed

![Figure 11.2-3d: Outer edge simply supported, inner edge fixed](figures/fig11_2_3d.png)
<!-- Description: Two schematic cross-sectional diagrams of a flat circular plate with linearly increasing load q from b to a, showing inner radius r_o, simply supported outer edge, and fixed inner edge. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0$

$$
M_{rb}=-qa^2\frac{C_3L_{18}-C_9L_{12}}{C_2C_9-C_3C_8}
$$

$$
Q_b=qa\frac{C_2L_{18}-C_8L_{12}}{C_2C_9-C_3C_8}
$$

$$
\theta_a=M_{rb}\frac{a}{D}C_5+Q_b\frac{a^2}{D}C_6-\frac{qa^3}{D}L_{15}
$$

$$
Q_a=Q_b\frac{b}{a}-\frac{q}{6a}\left(2a^2-r_oa-r_o^2\right)

## 3e. Outer edge fixed, inner edge free

![Figure 11.2-3e: Outer edge fixed, inner edge free](figures/fig11_2_3e.png)
<!-- Description: Two schematic cross-sectional diagrams of a flat circular plate with linearly increasing load q from b to a, showing inner radius r_o, fixed outer edge, and free inner edge. -->

$M_{rb} = 0,\quad Q_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0$

$$
y_b=\frac{-qa^4}{D}\left(\frac{C_1L_{15}}{C_4}-L_{12}\right)
$$

$$
\theta_b=\frac{qa^3L_{15}}{DC_4}
$$

$$
M_{ra}=-qa^2\left(L_{18}-\frac{C_7}{C_4}L_{15}\right)
$$

$$
Q_a=\frac{-q}{6a}\left(2a^2-r_oa-r_o^2\right)
