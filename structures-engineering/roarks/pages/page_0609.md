Meridional radial shear $=V=-y_A\,2D\lambda^3F_2-\psi_A\,2D\lambda^2F_3+LT_V$

Meridional bending moment $=M=-y_A\,2D\lambda^2F_3-\psi_A\,D\lambda F_4+LT_M$

Meridional slope $=\psi=\psi_AF_1-y_A\lambda F_4+LT_\psi$

Radial deflection $=y=y_AF_1+\dfrac{\psi_A}{2\lambda}F_2+LT_y$

Circumferential membrane stress $=\sigma_2=\dfrac{yE}{R}+v\sigma_1$

Meridional bending stress $=\sigma_1'=\dfrac{-6M}{t^2}$

Circumferential bending stress $=\sigma_2'=v\sigma_1'$

Meridional radial shear stress $=\tau=\dfrac{V}{t}$ (average value)
