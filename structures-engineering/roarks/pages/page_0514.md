## 8b. Uniform over small concentric circle of radius $r_0$ (note definition of $r_0'$)

(At center)
$$
\sigma_b=\frac{3W}{2\pi t^2}\left[\left(1+\nu\right)\ln\frac{2b}{\pi r_0'}+\beta_1\right]
\qquad \text{and} \qquad
y_{\max}=\frac{\alpha W b^2}{E t^3}
$$

(At center of long edge)
$$
\sigma_b=-\frac{\beta_2 W}{t^2}
$$
