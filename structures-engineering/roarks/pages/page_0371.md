**3g.** Right end supported and slope-guided, left end supported and roll-guided.

![Figure 3g](figures/fig3g.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with end forces and moments labeled $V_A$, $T_A$ at the left end and $V_B$, $M_B$ at the right end, with applied load $T_0$. Boundary conditions shown below the figure. -->

$M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0$  
$T_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0$

$$
V_A=-\frac{T_o}{R}\frac{C_{a2}\cos^2\phi-C_{a5}\sin\phi\cos\phi+(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+C_3\cos^2\phi-C_6\sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
T_A=-T_o\frac{(C_{a5}\sin\phi-C_{a2}\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_3\cos\phi-C_6\sin\phi)\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+C_3\cos^2\phi-C_6\sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=-\frac{T_oR}{EI}\frac{(C_{a2}C_5-C_{a5}C_2)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_{a5}C_3-C_{a2}C_6)\cos\phi+(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+C_3\cos^2\phi-C_6\sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi-T_A\sin\phi-T_o\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\psi_B=-\Theta_A\sin\phi+\frac{T_AR}{EI}C_8+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9+\frac{T_oR}{EI}C_{a8}
$$

**3h.** Right end supported and slope-guided, left end simply supported

![Figure 3h](figures/fig3h.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with left end simply supported and right end supported and slope-guided, showing forces $V_A$, $V_B$, moment $M_B$, and applied load $T_0$. Boundary conditions shown below the figure. -->

$M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0$  
$T_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0$

$$
V_A=-\frac{T_o\cos(\phi-\theta)}{R(1-\cos\phi)}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=-\frac{T_oR}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a2}\sin\phi+C_6\cos(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi}-\frac{C_3\sin\phi\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}-C_{a5}\right]
$$

$$
\psi_A=-\frac{T_oR}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a5}\sin\phi-C_{a2}\cos\phi}{1-\cos\phi}+(C_3\cos\phi-C_6\sin\phi)\frac{\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}\right]
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi-T_o\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\psi_B=\psi_A\cos\phi-\Theta_A\sin\phi+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9+\frac{T_oR}{EI}C_{a8}
$$
