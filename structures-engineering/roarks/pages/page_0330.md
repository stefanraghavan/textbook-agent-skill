The radial pressure $w_x$ varies with $(x-\theta)^2$ from 0 at $x=\theta$ to $w$ at $x=\pi$

$$
LT_M=\frac{-wR^2}{(\pi-\theta)^2}\big[(x-\theta)^2-2+2uc+2zs\big]\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

$$
LT_N=\frac{-wR}{(\pi-\theta)^2}\big[(x-\theta)^2-2+2uc+2zs\big]\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

$$
LT_V=\frac{-2wR}{(\pi-\theta)^2}(x-\theta-zc+us)\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

$$
M_A=\frac{-wR^2}{\pi(\pi-\theta)^2}\left\{2(\pi-\theta)(2-c)-6s+k_2\left[2(\pi-\theta-s)-\frac{(\pi-\theta)^3}{3}\right]\right\}
$$

$$
M_C=\frac{-wR^2}{\pi(\pi-\theta)^2}\left\{2\theta(2-c)+6s-6\pi+\pi(\pi-\theta)^2+k_2\left[2(\pi-\theta-s)-\frac{(\pi-\theta)^3}{3}\right]\right\}
$$

$$
N_A=\frac{-wR}{\pi(\pi-\theta)^2}\big[2(\pi-\theta)(2-c)-6s\big]
$$

$$
V_A=0
$$

$$
\Delta D_H=
\begin{cases}
\displaystyle
\frac{-wR^4}{EI(\pi-\theta)^2}\left\{
k_1\left(\frac{\pi^2}{4}-6-\theta^2s+3s-3\theta c+\frac{3\pi}{2}+c+\theta s-2\theta\right)
+k_2\left(\frac{\pi^2}{2}-4+4s-2\theta\pi+2\theta^2\right)
-2k_2^2\frac{(\pi-\theta)^3-6(\pi-\theta-s)}{3\pi}
\right\}
&
\text{for } \theta\le \frac{\pi}{2}
\\[1.2em]
\displaystyle
\frac{-wR^4}{EI(\pi-\theta)^2}\left\{
k_1[(2-c)(\pi-\theta)-3s]
-2k_2^2\frac{(\pi-\theta)^3-6(\pi-\theta-s)}{3\pi}
\right\}
&
\text{for } \theta\ge \frac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\Delta D_V=\frac{wR^4}{EI(\pi-\theta)^2}\left\{
k_1[2+2c-s(\pi-\theta)]
+k_2(2+2c-(\pi-\theta)^2)
+2k_2^2\frac{(\pi-\theta)^3-6(\pi-\theta-s)}{3\pi}
\right\}
$$

Max $+\,M$ occurs at an angular position $x_1$

where $x_1>\theta$, $x_1>108.6^\circ$, and $x_1$ is found from

$$
(x_1-\theta+s\cos x_1)+(3s-2\pi+2\theta-\theta c)\sin x_1=0
$$

Max $-\,M=M_C$
