**4j. Right end supported and roll-guided, left end simply supported**

![Figure 9.4j: Right end supported and roll-guided, left end simply supported](figures/fig9_4j.png)

$$
V_A = wR\frac{1-\cos(\phi-\theta)}{\sin\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = \frac{wR^3}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a13}\cos\phi - C_{a19}(1-\cos\phi)}{\sin\phi}
-\left[C_3\cos\phi - C_9(1-\cos\phi)\right]\frac{1-\cos(\phi-\theta)}{\sin^2\phi}\right]
$$

$$
\psi_A = \frac{wR^3}{EI}\left[C_{a13}+C_{a19}-(C_3+C_9)\frac{1-\cos(\phi-\theta)}{\sin\phi}\right]
$$

$$
V_B = V_A - wR(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B = V_AR(1-\cos\phi)-wR^2[\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
\Theta_B = \Theta_A\cos\phi + \psi_A\sin\phi + \frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_6 - \frac{wR^3}{EI}C_{a16}
$$

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0
$$

$$
M_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

**5. Uniformly distributed torque**

![Figure 9.5: Uniformly distributed torque](figures/fig9_5.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam segment with end forces and moments at A and B, and uniformly distributed torque t_0 along the beam. Angular parameters x, theta, and phi are indicated. -->

Transverse shear
$$
V = V_A
$$

Bending moment
$$
M = V_AR\sin x + M_A\cos x - T_A\sin x - t_0R[1-\cos(x-\theta)]\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

Twisting moment
$$
T = V_AR(1-\cos x) + M_A\sin x + T_A\cos x + t_0R\sin(x-\theta)\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

Vertical deflection
$$
y = y_A + \Theta_AR\sin x + \psi_AR(1-\cos x) + \frac{M_AR^2}{EI}F_1 + \frac{T_AR^2}{EI}F_2 + \frac{V_AR^3}{EI}F_3 + \frac{t_0R^3}{EI}F_{a12}
$$

Bending slope
$$
\Theta = \Theta_A\cos x + \psi_A\sin x + \frac{M_AR}{EI}F_4 + \frac{T_AR}{EI}F_5 + \frac{V_AR^2}{EI}F_6 + \frac{t_0R^2}{EI}F_{a15}
$$

Roll slope
$$
\psi = \psi_A\cos x - \Theta_A\sin x + \frac{M_AR}{EI}F_7 + \frac{T_AR}{EI}F_8 + \frac{V_AR^2}{EI}F_9 + \frac{t_0R^2}{EI}F_{a18}
$$

For tabulated values:
$$
V = K_Vt_0,\qquad M = K_Mt_0R,\qquad T = K_Tt_0R,\qquad y = K_y\frac{t_0R^3}{EI},\qquad \Theta = K_\Theta\frac{t_0R^2}{EI},\qquad \psi = K_\psi\frac{t_0R^2}{EI}
$$
