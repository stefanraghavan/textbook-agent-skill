**5g.** Right end supported and slope-guided, left end supported and roll-guided

![Figure 5g](figures/fig5g.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with end forces and moments labeled $V_A$, $T_A$ at the left end and $V_B$, $M_B$ at the right end, with distributed loading $t_0$ along the curved member. Boundary conditions shown: $M_A=0$, $y_A=0$, $\psi_A=0$, $T_B=0$, $y_B=0$, $\Theta_B=0$. -->

$$
V_A=-t_0\frac{C_{a12}\cos^2\phi-C_{a15}\sin\phi\cos\phi+(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+C_3\cos^2\phi-C_6\sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
T_A=-t_0R\frac{(C_{a15}\sin\phi-C_{a12}\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_3\cos\phi-C_6\sin\phi)\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+C_3\cos^2\phi-C_6\sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=-\frac{t_0R^2}{EI}\frac{(C_{a12}C_5-C_{a15}C_2)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_{a15}C_3-C_{a12}C_6)\cos\phi+(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(C_5\sin\phi-C_2\cos\phi)(1-\cos\phi)+C_3\cos^2\phi-C_6\sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi-T_A\sin\phi-t_0R[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
\psi_B=-\Theta_A\sin\phi+\frac{T_AR}{EI}C_8+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9+\frac{t_0R^2}{EI}C_{a18}
$$

$$
M_A=0 \qquad y_A=0 \qquad \psi_A=0
$$

$$
T_B=0 \qquad y_B=0 \qquad \Theta_B=0
$$

**5h.** Right end supported and slope-guided, left end simply supported

![Figure 5h](figures/fig5h.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with end force $V_A$ at the left end and $V_B$, $M_B$ at the right end, with distributed loading $t_0$ along the curved member. Boundary conditions shown: $M_A=0$, $T_A=0$, $y_A=0$, $T_B=0$, $y_B=0$, $\Theta_B=0$. -->

$$
V_A=-\frac{t_0\sin(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=-\frac{t_0R^2}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a12}\sin\phi+C_6\sin(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi}-\frac{C_3\sin\phi\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}-C_{a15}\right]
$$

$$
\psi_A=-\frac{t_0R^2}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a15}\sin\phi-C_{a12}\cos\phi}{1-\cos\phi}+(C_3\cos\phi-C_6\sin\phi)\frac{\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}\right]
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi-t_0R[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
\psi_B=\psi_A\cos\phi-\Theta_A\sin\phi+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9+\frac{t_0R^2}{EI}C_{a18}
$$

$$
M_A=0 \qquad T_A=0 \qquad y_A=0
$$

$$
T_B=0 \qquad y_B=0 \qquad \Theta_B=0
$$
