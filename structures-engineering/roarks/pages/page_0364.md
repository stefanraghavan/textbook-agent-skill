## 2d. Right end fixed, left end supported and roll-guided

![Figure 2d](figures/fig2d.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with right end fixed and left end supported and roll-guided, showing applied moment $M_o$ and reactions $T_A$ and $V_A$. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0
$$

$$
y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A=-\frac{M_o}{R}\frac{[(C_{a1}+C_{a7})C_5-C_{a4}(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_{a1}C_8-C_{a7}C_2)\cos\phi}{[C_5(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
T_A=-M_o\frac{[C_{a4}(C_3+C_9)-(C_{a1}+C_{a7})C_6]\sin\phi+(C_{a7}C_3-C_{a1}C_9)\cos\phi}{[C_5(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=-\frac{M_oR}{EI}\frac{C_{a1}C_5C_9-C_6C_3)+C_{a4}(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_{a7}(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}{[C_5(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi-T_A\sin\phi+M_o\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B=V_AR(1-\cos\phi)+T_A\cos\phi+M_o\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

## 2e. Right end fixed, left end fixed

![Figure 2e](figures/fig2e.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with both ends fixed, showing applied moment $M_o$. -->

$$
y_A = 0 \qquad \Theta_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0
$$

$$
y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A=-\frac{M_o}{R}\frac{C_{a1}(C_4C_8-C_5C_7)+C_{a4}(C_2C_7-C_1C_8)+C_{a7}(C_1C_5-C_2C_4)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
M_A=-M_o\frac{C_{a1}(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_{a4}(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_{a7}(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
T_A=-M_o\frac{C_{a1}(C_6C_7-C_4C_9)+C_{a4}(C_1C_9-C_3C_7)+C_{a7}(C_3C_4-C_1C_6)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi+M_A\cos\phi-T_A\sin\phi+M_o\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B=V_AR(1-\cos\phi)+M_A\sin\phi+T_A\cos\phi+M_o\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$
