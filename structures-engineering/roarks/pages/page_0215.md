## 8. Left end guided vertically, right end fixed

![Figure 8.2-8: Left end guided vertically, right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_8.png)
<!-- Description: Frame diagram showing the left end guided vertically and the right end fixed, with labeled actions $M_A$, $H_A$, $M_B$, $V_B$, and $H_B$. -->

Since $\delta_{HA}=0$, $\psi_A=0$, and $V_A=0$,

$$
H_A=\frac{LF_HC_{MM}-LF_MC_{HM}}{C_{HH}C_{MM}-(C_{HM})^2}
\qquad
M_A=\frac{LF_MC_{HH}-LF_HC_{HM}}{C_{HH}C_{MM}-(C_{HM})^2}
$$

$$
\delta_{VA}=C_{VH}H_A+C_{VM}M_A-LF_V
$$

Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r

## 9. Left end roller supported along the horizontal, right end fixed

![Figure 8.2-9: Left end roller supported along the horizontal, right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_9.png)
<!-- Description: Frame diagram showing the left end on a horizontal roller support and the right end fixed, with labeled actions $V_A$, $M_B$, $V_B$, and $H_B$. -->

Since $\delta_{VA}=0$, $H_A=0$, and $M_A=0$,

$$
V_A=\frac{LF_V}{C_{VV}}
\qquad
\delta_{HA}=C_{HV}V_A-LF_H
\qquad
\text{and}
\qquad
\psi_A=C_{MV}V_A-LF_M
$$

Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r

---

## 10. Left end roller supported along the vertical, right end fixed

![Figure 8.2-10: Left end roller supported along the vertical, right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_10.png)
<!-- Description: Frame diagram showing the left end on a vertical roller support and the right end fixed, with labeled actions $H_A$, $M_B$, $V_B$, and $H_B$. -->

Since $\delta_{HA}=0$, $V_A=0$, and $M_A=0$,

$$
H_A=\frac{LF_H}{C_{HH}}
\qquad
\delta_{VA}=C_{VH}H_A-LF_V
\qquad
\text{and}
\qquad
\psi_A=C_{MH}H_A-LF_M
$$

Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r

## 11. Left end guided by moment only (zero slope at the left end), right end fixed

![Figure 8.2-11: Left end guided by moment only (zero slope at the left end), right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_11.png)
<!-- Description: Frame diagram showing the left end guided by moment only with zero slope and the right end fixed, with labeled actions $M_A$, $M_B$, $V_B$, and $H_B$. -->

$$

\qquad
\delta_{HA}=C_{HM}M_A-LF_H
\qquad
\text{and}
\qquad
\delta_{VA}=C_{VM}M_A-LF_V
$$

Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r

## 12. Left end free, right end fixed

![Figure 8.2-12: Left end free, right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_12.png)
<!-- Description: Frame diagram showing the left end free and the right end fixed, with labeled actions $M_B$, $V_B$, and $H_B$. -->

Since $H_A=0$, $V_A=0$ and $M_A=0$, this is a statically determinate case. The deflections are given by

$$
\delta_{HA}=-LF_H
\qquad
\delta_{VA}=-LF_V
\qquad
\text{and}
\qquad
\psi_A=-LF_M
$$

Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r
