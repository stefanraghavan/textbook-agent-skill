General expressions for deformations, moments, and shears:

$$
y=y_b+\theta_b rF_1+M_{rb}\frac{r^2}{D}F_2+Q_b\frac{r^3}{D}F_3-w\frac{r_o^3}{D}G_3

$$
\theta=\theta_bF_4+M_{rb}\frac{r}{D}F_5+Q_b\frac{r^2}{D}F_6-w\frac{r_o^2}{D}G_6
$$

$$
M_r=\theta_b\frac{D}{r}F_7+M_{rb}F_8+Q_b rF_9-wrG_9
$$

$$
M_t=\frac{\theta D(1-\nu^2)}{r}+\nu M_r
$$

$$
Q=Q_b\frac{b}{r}-w\frac{r_o}{r}(r-r_o)^0
$$

$$
y=K_y\frac{wa^3}{D},\qquad \theta=K_\theta\frac{wa^2}{D},\qquad M=K_Mwa,\qquad Q=K_Qw
