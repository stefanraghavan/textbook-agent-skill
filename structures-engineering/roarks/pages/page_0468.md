# TABLE 11.2 Formulas for flat circular plates of constant thickness *(Continued)*

**1k. Outer edge free, inner edge simply supported**

![Figure 11.2-1k: Outer edge free, inner edge simply supported](figures/fig11_2_1k.png)
<!-- Description: Annular plate sketch showing inner radius $r_o$, load $w$, and edge condition diagram for outer edge free and inner edge simply supported. -->

$$
y_b=0,\qquad M_{rb}=0,\qquad M_{ra}=0,\qquad Q_a=0
$$

$$
\theta_b=-\frac{wa^2}{DC_7}\left(\frac{r_oC_9}{b}-L_9\right)
$$

$$
Q_b=\frac{wr_o}{b}
$$

$$
y_a=-\frac{wa^3}{D}\left[\frac{C_1}{C_7}\left(\frac{r_oC_9}{b}-L_9\right)-\frac{r_oC_3}{b}+L_3\right]
$$

$$
\theta_a=-\frac{wa^2}{D}\left[\frac{C_4}{C_7}\left(\frac{r_oC_9}{b}-L_9\right)-\frac{r_oC_6}{b}+L_6\right]
$$

If $r_o=a$ (load at outer edge),

$$
y_{\max}=y_a=-\frac{wa^4}{bD}\left(\frac{C_1C_9}{C_7}-C_3\right)
$$

$$
M_{\max}=M_{tb}=-\frac{wa^3}{b^2}(1-\nu^2)\frac{C_9}{C_7}
$$

(For numerical values see case 1a after computing the loading at the inner edge)

**1l. Outer edge free, inner edge fixed**

![Figure 11.2-1l: Outer edge free, inner edge fixed](figures/fig11_2_1l.png)
<!-- Description: Annular plate sketch showing inner radius $r_o$, load $w$, and edge condition diagram for outer edge free and inner edge fixed. -->

$$
y_b=0,\qquad \theta_b=0,\qquad M_{ra}=0,\qquad Q_a=0
$$

$$
M_{rb}=-\frac{wa}{C_8}\left(\frac{r_oC_9}{b}-L_9\right)
$$

$$
Q_b=\frac{wr_o}{b}
$$

$$
y_a=-\frac{wa^3}{D}\left[\frac{C_2}{C_8}\left(\frac{r_oC_9}{b}-L_9\right)-\frac{r_oC_3}{b}+L_3\right]
$$

$$
\theta_a=-\frac{wa^2}{D}\left[\frac{C_5}{C_8}\left(\frac{r_oC_9}{b}-L_9\right)-\frac{r_oC_6}{b}+L_6\right]
$$

If $r_o=a$ (load at outer edge),

$$
y_{\max}=y_a=-\frac{wa^4}{bD}\left(\frac{C_2C_9}{C_8}-C_3\right)
$$

$$
M_{\max}=M_{rb}=-\frac{wa^2}{b}\frac{C_9}{C_8}
$$

(For numerical values see case 1b after computing the loading at the inner edge)

## Case 2. Annular plate with a uniformly distributed pressure $q$ over the portion from $r_o$ to $a$

![Figure 11.2-Case2: Annular plate with uniformly distributed pressure over outer portion](figures/fig11_2_case2.png)
<!-- Description: Diagram of an annular plate with dimensions $a$, $b$, and $r_o$, distributed load $q$ acting over the portion from $r_o$ to $a$, and notation for deflections, rotations, moments, and shears at inner and outer boundaries. -->

General expressions for deformations, moments, and shears:

$$
y=y_b+\theta_brF_1+M_{rb}\frac{r^2}{D}F_2+Q_b\frac{r^3}{D}F_3-q\frac{r^4}{D}G_{11}
$$

$$
\theta=\theta_bF_4+M_{rb}\frac{r}{D}F_5+Q_b\frac{r^2}{D}F_6-q\frac{r^3}{D}G_{14}
$$

$$
M_r=\theta_b\frac{D}{r}F_7+M_{rb}F_8+Q_brF_9-qr^2G_{17}
$$

$$
M_t=\frac{\partial D(1-\nu^2)}{r}\theta+\nu M_r
$$

$$
Q=Q_b\frac{b}{r}-\frac{q}{2r}(r^2-r_o^2)(r-r_o)^0
$$

For the numerical data given below, $\nu=0.3$

$$
y=K_y\frac{qa^4}{D},\qquad \theta=K_\theta\frac{qa^3}{D},\qquad M=K_Mqa^2,\qquad Q=K_Qqa
$$
