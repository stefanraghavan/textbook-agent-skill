# TABLE A.1 Properties of sections *(Continued)*

## 4. Tee section

![Figure 4: Tee section](figures/fig4.png)
<!-- Description: Tee section diagram with dimensions b, t, d, t_w and centroid/axes labeled x, y, x_c, y_c. -->

$$
A = tb + t_w d
$$

$$
y_c = \frac{bt^2 + t_w d(2t + d)}{2(tb + t_w d)}
$$

$$
x_c = \frac{b}{2}
$$

$$
I_x = \frac{b}{3}(d+t)^3 - \frac{d^3}{3}(b-t_w) - A(d+t-y_c)^2
$$

$$
I_y = \frac{tb^3}{12} + \frac{dt_w^3}{12}
$$

$$
r_x = \left(\frac{I_x}{A}\right)^{1/2}
$$

$$
r_y = \left(\frac{I_y}{A}\right)^{1/2}
$$

If $t_w d \ge bt$, then

$$
Z_x = \frac{d^2 t_w}{2} - \frac{b^2 t^2}{4t_w} + \frac{bt(d+t)}{2}
$$

Neutral axis $x$ is located a distance $(bt/t_w + d)/2$ from the bottom.

If $t_w d \le bt$, then

$$
Z_x = \frac{t^2 b}{4} + \frac{t_w d\left(t + d - t_w d/2b\right)}{2}
$$

Neutral axis $x$ is located a distance $(t_w d/b + t)/2$ from the top.

$$
\mathrm{SF}_x = \frac{Z_x(d+t-y_c)}{I_x}
$$

$$
Z_y = \frac{b^2 t + t_w^2 d}{4}
$$

$$
\mathrm{SF}_y = \frac{Z_y b}{2I_y}
$$

## 5. Channel section

![Figure 5: Channel section](figures/fig5.png)
<!-- Description: Channel section diagram with dimensions b, t, d, t_w and centroid/axes labeled x, y, x_c, y_c. -->

$$
A = tb + 2t_w d
$$

$$
y_c = \frac{bt^2 + 2t_w d(2t + d)}{2(tb + 2t_w d)}
$$

$$
x_c = \frac{b}{2}
$$

$$
I_x = \frac{b}{3}(d+t)^3 - \frac{d^3}{3}(b-2t_w) - A(d+t-y_c)^2
$$

$$
I_y = \frac{(d+t)b^3}{12} - \frac{d(b-2t_w)^3}{12}
$$

$$
r_x = \left(\frac{I_x}{A}\right)^{1/2}
$$

$$
r_y = \left(\frac{I_y}{A}\right)^{1/2}
$$

If $2t_w d \ge bt$, then

$$
Z_x = \frac{d^2 t_w}{2} - \frac{b^2 t^2}{8t_w} + \frac{bt(d+t)}{2}
$$

Neutral axis $x$ is located a distance $(bt/2t_w + d)/2$ from the bottom.

If $2t_w d \le bt$, then

$$
Z_x = \frac{t^2 b}{4} + t_w d\left(t + d - \frac{t_w d}{b}\right)
$$

Neutral axis $x$ is located a distance $t_w d/b + t/2$ from the top.

$$
\mathrm{SF}_x = \frac{Z_x(d+t-y_c)}{I_x}
$$

$$
Z_y = \frac{b^2 t}{4} + t_w d(b-t_w)
$$

$$
\mathrm{SF}_y = \frac{Z_y b}{2I_y}
$$
