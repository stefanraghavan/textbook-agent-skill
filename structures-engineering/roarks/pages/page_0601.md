Uniform loading, force/unit area; on the horizontal projected area; tangential top edge support | $\sigma_1=\dfrac{wR}{2t\cos\alpha}$  <br> $\sigma_2=\dfrac{wR\sin^2\alpha}{t\cos\alpha}$  <br> $\Delta R=\dfrac{wR^2}{Et\cos\alpha}\left(\sin^2\alpha-\dfrac{\nu}{2}\right)$  <br> $\Delta y=\dfrac{wR^2}{2Et\cos^2\alpha}\left[\dfrac{1}{2\sin\alpha}+\nu(1-\sin\alpha)-2\sin^2\alpha\right]$  <br> $\psi=\dfrac{wR\sin\alpha}{2Et\cos^2\alpha}\left(4\sin^2\alpha-1-2\nu\cos^2\alpha\right)$ |
