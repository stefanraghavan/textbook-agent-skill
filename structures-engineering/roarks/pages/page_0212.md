Distributed load on the horizontal member | ![Figure 5b](figures/fig5b.png) <!-- Description: Frame with distributed load on the horizontal member, varying from $w_a$ to $w_b$. --> | $LF_H = w_a \left[\dfrac{l_2 l_2^3}{4E_2 I_2}(2l_1 - l_2) + \dfrac{l_1 l_3^3}{6E_3 I_3}\right] + (w_b - w_a)\left[\dfrac{l_2 l_2^3}{12E_2 I_2}(2l_1 - l_2) + \dfrac{l_1 l_3^3}{24E_3 I_3}\right]$  <br><br> $LF_V = w_a \left(\dfrac{l_2 l_3^3}{2E_2 I_2} + \dfrac{l_1^4}{8E_3 I_3}\right) + (w_b - w_a)\left(\dfrac{l_2 l_3^3}{6E_2 I_2} + \dfrac{l_1^4}{30E_3 I_3}\right)$  <br><br> $LF_M = w_a \left(\dfrac{l_2 l_3^3}{2E_2 I_2} + \dfrac{l_1^3}{6E_3 I_3}\right) + (w_b - w_a)\left(\dfrac{l_2 l_3^3}{6E_2 I_2} + \dfrac{l_1^3}{24E_3 I_3}\right)$ |
| 5c. Concentrated moment on the horizontal member | ![Figure 5c](figures/fig5c.png) <!-- Description: Frame with a concentrated moment $M_0$ applied on the horizontal member at distance $a$. --> | $LF_H = M_0\left[\dfrac{-l_2}{2E_2 I_2}(2l_1 - l_2) - \dfrac{l_1}{E_3 I_3}(l_3 - a)\right]$  <br><br> $LF_V = M_0\left(-C_{VM} + \dfrac{a^2}{2E_3 I_3}\right)$  <br><br> $LF_M = M_0\left[\dfrac{-l_2}{E_2 I_2} - \dfrac{1}{E_3 I_3}(l_3 - a)\right]$ |
| 5d. Concentrated angular displacement on the horizontal member | ![Figure 5d](figures/fig5d.png) <!-- Description: Frame with concentrated angular displacement $\theta_0$ on the horizontal member at distance $a$. --> | $LF_H = \theta_0(l_1)$  <br><br> $LF_V = \theta_0(a)$  <br><br> $LF_M = \theta_0(1)$ |
| 5e. Concentrated lateral displacement on the horizontal member | ![Figure 5e](figures/fig5e.png) <!-- Description: Frame with concentrated lateral displacement $\Delta_0$ on the horizontal member at distance $a$. --> | $LF_H = 0$  <br><br> $LF_V = \Delta_0(1)$  <br><br> $LF_M = 0$ |
| 5f. Concentrated load on the left vertical member | ![Figure 5f](figures/fig5f.png) <!-- Description: Frame with concentrated load $W$ on the left vertical member at distance $a$. --> | $LF_H = W\left(C_{HH} - aC_{MH} + \dfrac{a^3}{6E_1 I_1}\right)$  <br><br> $LF_V = W(C_{VH} - aC_{VM})$  <br><br> $LF_M = W\left(C_{MH} - aC_{MM} + \dfrac{a^2}{2E_1 I_1}\right)$ |
| 5g. Distributed load on the left vertical member | ![Figure 5g](figures/fig5g.png) <!-- Description: Frame with distributed load on the left vertical member, varying from $w_a$ to $w_b$. --> | $LF_H = w_a\left(C_{HH}l_1 - C_{MH}\dfrac{l_1^2}{2} + \dfrac{l_1^4}{24E_1 I_1}\right) + (w_b - w_a)\left(C_{HH}\dfrac{l_1}{2} - C_{MH}\dfrac{l_1^2}{3} + \dfrac{l_1^4}{30E_1 I_1}\right)$  <br><br> $LF_V = w_a\left(C_{VH}l_1 - C_{VM}\dfrac{l_1^2}{2}\right) + (w_b - w_a)\left(C_{VH}\dfrac{l_1}{2} - C_{VM}\dfrac{l_1^2}{3}\right)$  <br><br> $LF_M = w_a\left(C_{MH}l_1 - C_{MM}\dfrac{l_1^2}{2} + \dfrac{l_1^3}{6E_1 I_1}\right) + (w_b - w_a)\left(C_{MH}\dfrac{l_1}{2} - C_{MM}\dfrac{l_1^2}{3} + \dfrac{l_1^3}{8E_1 I_1}\right)$ |
