**4d. Right end fixed, left end supported and roll-guided**

![Figure 4d](figures/fig4d.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with distributed load $w$ acting downward near the right end; left end supported and roll-guided with reactions $T_A$ and $V_A$ shown; right end fixed. Boundary conditions shown below the figure. -->

$M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0$  
$y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0$

$$
V_A=wR\frac{[(C_{a13}+C_{a19})C_5-C_{a16}(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_{a13}C_8-C_{a19}C_2)\cos\phi}{[C_5(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
T_A=wR^2\frac{[C_{a16}(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_{a13}+C_{a19})]\sin\phi+(C_{a19}C_3-C_{a13}C_9)\cos\phi}{[C_5(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=\frac{wR^3}{EI}\frac{C_{a13}(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_{a16}(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_{a19}(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}{[C_5(C_3+C_9)-C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi+(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A-wR(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
M_B=V_A R\sin\phi-T_A\sin\phi-wR^2[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
T_B=V_A R(1-\cos\phi)+T_A\cos\phi-wR^2[\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)]
$$

**4e. Right end fixed, left end fixed**

![Figure 4e](figures/fig4e.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with distributed load $w$ acting downward near the right end; both left and right ends fixed. Boundary conditions shown below the figure. -->

$y_A = 0 \qquad \Theta_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0$  
$y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0$

$$
V_A=wR\frac{C_{a13}(C_4C_8-C_5C_7)+C_{a16}(C_2C_7-C_1C_8)+C_{a19}(C_1C_5-C_2C_4)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
M_A=wR^2\frac{C_{a13}(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_{a16}(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_{a19}(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
T_A=wR^2\frac{C_{a13}(C_6C_7-C_4C_9)+C_{a16}(C_1C_9-C_3C_7)+C_{a19}(C_3C_4-C_1C_6)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A-wR(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
M_B=V_A R\sin\phi+M_A\cos\phi-T_A\sin\phi-wR^2[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
