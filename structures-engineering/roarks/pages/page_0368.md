# TABLE 9.4 Formulas for curved beams of compact cross section loaded normal to the plane of curvature *(Continued)*

**3a. Right end fixed, left end free**

![Figure 9.4-3a: Right end fixed, left end free](figures/fig9_4_3a.png)
<!-- Description: A curved beam with the right end fixed and the left end free, subjected to applied torque $T_0$. Boundary conditions and end actions are shown. -->

$$
V_A = 0 \qquad M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0
$$

$$
y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
y_A = \frac{T_0 R^2}{EI}\left[C_{a5}\sin\phi - C_{a8}(1-\cos\phi) - C_{a2}\right]
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{T_0 R}{EI}\left(C_{a5}\cos\phi - C_{a8}\sin\phi\right)
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{T_0 R}{EI}\left(C_{a8}\cos\phi + C_{a5}\sin\phi\right)
$$

$$
V_B = 0
$$

$$
M_B = -T_0\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B = T_0\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

**3b. Right end fixed, left end simply supported**

![Figure 9.4-3b: Right end fixed, left end simply supported](figures/fig9_4_3b.png)
<!-- Description: A curved beam with the right end fixed and the left end simply supported, subjected to applied torque $T_0$. A vertical reaction $V_A$ is shown at the left end, with boundary conditions and end actions indicated. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0
$$

$$
y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = -\frac{T_0}{R}\frac{C_{a8}(1-\cos\phi)-C_{a5}\sin\phi+C_{a2}}{C_9(1-\cos\phi)-C_6\sin\phi+C_3}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{T_0 R}{EI}\frac{(C_{a2}C_9-C_{a8}C_3)\sin\phi+(C_{a8}C_6-C_{a5}C_9)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_{a5}C_3-C_{a2}C_6)\cos\phi}{C_9(1-\cos\phi)-C_6\sin\phi+C_3}
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{T_0 R}{EI}\frac{\left[C_{a5}(C_9+C_3)-C_6(C_{a2}+C_{a8})\right]\sin\phi+(C_{a8}C_3-C_{a2}C_9)\cos\phi}{C_9(1-\cos\phi)-C_6\sin\phi+C_3}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
M_B = V_A R\sin\phi - T_0\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B = V_A R(1-\cos\phi) + T_0\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$
