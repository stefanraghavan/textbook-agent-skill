## 5c. Right end fixed, left end supported and slope-guided

![Figure 5c](figures/fig5c.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with right end fixed, left end supported and slope-guided, showing applied distributed load $t_0$, shear $V_A$, and moment $M_A$. -->

$$
T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \Theta_A = 0
$$

$$
y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = -t_o \frac{(C_{a18}C_4 - C_{a15}C_7)(1-\cos\phi) + (C_{a15}C_1 - C_{a12}C_4)\cos\phi + (C_{a12}C_7 - C_{a18}C_1)\sin\phi}{(C_4C_9 - C_6C_7)(1-\cos\phi) + (C_1C_6 - C_3C_4)\cos\phi + (C_3C_7 - C_1C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
M_A = -t_oR \frac{(C_{a15}C_9 - C_{a18}C_6)(1-\cos\phi) + (C_{a12}C_6 - C_{a15}C_3)\cos\phi + (C_{a18}C_3 - C_{a12}C_9)\sin\phi}{(C_4C_9 - C_6C_7)(1-\cos\phi) + (C_1C_6 - C_3C_4)\cos\phi + (C_3C_7 - C_1C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{t_oR^2}{EI} \frac{C_{a12}(C_4C_9 - C_6C_7) + C_{a15}(C_3C_7 - C_1C_9) + C_{a18}(C_1C_6 - C_3C_4)}{(C_4C_9 - C_6C_7)(1-\cos\phi) + (C_1C_6 - C_3C_4)\cos\phi + (C_3C_7 - C_1C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
M_B = V_AR\sin\phi + M_A\cos\phi - t_oR[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
T_B = V_AR(1-\cos\phi) + M_A\sin\phi + t_oR\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

## 5d. Right end fixed, left end supported and roll-guided

![Figure 5d](figures/fig5d.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with right end fixed, left end supported and roll-guided, showing applied distributed load $t_0$, shear $V_A$, and tangential reaction $T_A$. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0
$$

$$
y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = -t_o \frac{[(C_{a12}+C_{a18})C_5 - C_{a15}(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi + (C_{a12}C_8 - C_{a18}C_2)\cos\phi}{[C_5(C_3+C_9) - C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi + (C_3C_8 - C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
T_A = -t_oR \frac{[C_{a15}(C_3+C_9) - C_6(C_{a12}+C_{a18})]\sin\phi + (C_{a18}C_3 - C_{a12}C_9)\cos\phi}{[C_5(C_3+C_9) - C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi + (C_3C_8 - C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{t_oR^2}{EI} \frac{C_{a12}(C_5C_9 - C_6C_8) + C_{a15}(C_3C_8 - C_2C_9) + C_{a18}(C_2C_6 - C_3C_5)}{[C_5(C_3+C_9) - C_6(C_2+C_8)]\sin\phi + (C_3C_8 - C_2C_9)\cos\phi}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
M_B = V_AR\sin\phi - T_A\sin\phi - t_oR[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
T_B = V_AR(1-\cos\phi) + T_A\cos\phi + t_oR\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$
