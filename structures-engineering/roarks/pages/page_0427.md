| 2a. Left end free to twist and warp, right end free to warp but not twist

![Figure 10.3-2a](figures/fig10_3_2a.png)
<!-- Description: A thin-walled open member subjected to a uniformly distributed torque $t_0$ over the span, with the left end free to twist and warp and the right end restrained against twist but free to warp. --> | $T_A = 0,\quad \theta_A'' = 0$

$\theta_A=\dfrac{t_0}{2C_wE\beta^2}(l-a)^2$

$\theta_A'=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^3}\dfrac{C_{a3}}{C_2}$

$\theta_B=0,\quad \theta_B''=0$

$\theta_B'=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^3}\left[\dfrac{A_1-C_1}{C_2}+\beta(l-a)\right]$

$\theta_B'''=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta}\dfrac{A_1-C_1}{C_2}$

$T_B=-t_0(l-a)$ | If $a=0$ (uniformly distributed torque over entire span),

$\theta=\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^4}\left[\dfrac{\beta^2(l^2-x^2)}{2}+\dfrac{\sinh \beta(l-x)+\sinh \beta x}{\sinh \beta l}-1\right]$

$\theta'=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^3}\left[\beta x+\dfrac{\cosh \beta(l-x)-\cosh \beta x}{\sinh \beta l}\right]$

$\theta''=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^2}\left[1-\dfrac{\sinh \beta(l-x)+\sinh \beta x}{\sinh \beta l}\right]$

$\theta'''=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta}\dfrac{\cosh \beta(l-x)-\cosh \beta x}{\sinh \beta l}$

$\theta_{\max}=\dfrac{t_0l^2}{2C_wE\beta^2}\qquad \text{at } x=0$

$\theta'_{\max}=\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^3}\left(\beta l-\tanh \dfrac{\beta l}{2}\right)\qquad \text{at } x=l$

$\theta''_{\max}=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^2}\left[1-\dfrac{1}{\cosh (\beta l/2)}\right]\qquad \text{at } x=\dfrac{l}{2}$

$\theta'''_{\max}=\mp \dfrac{t_0}{C_wE}\tanh \dfrac{\beta l}{2}\qquad \text{at } x=0 \text{ and } x=l,\text{ respectively}$ |
| 2b. Left end free to twist and warp, right end fixed (no twist or warp)

![Figure 10.3-2b](figures/fig10_3_2b.png)
<!-- Description: A thin-walled open member subjected to a uniformly distributed torque $t_0$, with the left end free to twist and warp and the right end fixed against both twist and warp. --> | $T_A = 0,\quad \theta_A'' = 0$

$\theta_A=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^4}\left(\dfrac{C_2C_{a4}}{C_1}-C_{a5}\right)$

$\theta_A'=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^3}\dfrac{C_{a4}}{C_1}$

$\theta_B=0,\quad \theta_B'=0$

$\theta_B''=\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^2}\left(\dfrac{C_2C_{a4}}{C_1}-C_{a3}\right)$

$\theta_B'''=\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE}(l-a)$

$T_B=-t_0(l-a)$ | If $a=0$ (uniformly distributed torque over entire span)

$\theta=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^4}\left[\dfrac{1-\cosh \beta(l-x)+\beta l(\sinh \beta l-\sinh \beta x)}{\cosh \beta l}-\dfrac{\beta^2(l^2-x^2)}{2}\right]$

$\theta'=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^3}\left[\dfrac{\sinh \beta(l-x)-\beta l\cosh \beta x}{\cosh \beta l}+\beta x\right]$

$\theta''=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^2}\left[1-\dfrac{\cosh \beta(l-x)+\beta l\sinh \beta x}{\cosh \beta l}\right]$

$\theta'''=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta}\dfrac{\sinh \beta(l-x)-\beta l\cosh \beta x}{\cosh \beta l}$

$\theta_{\max}=-\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^4}\left(1+\dfrac{\beta^2l^2}{2}-\dfrac{1+\beta l\sinh \beta l}{\cosh \beta l}\right)\qquad \text{at } x=0$

The location of max $\theta'$ depends upon $\beta l$

$\theta''_{\max}=\dfrac{t_0}{C_wE\beta^2}\left(\dfrac{1+\beta l\sinh \beta l}{\cosh \beta l}-1\right)\qquad \text{at } x=l$

$\theta'''_{\max}=\dfrac{t_0l}{C_wE}\qquad \text{at } x=l$ |
