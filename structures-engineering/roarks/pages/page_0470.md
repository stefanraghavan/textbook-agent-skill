## 2d. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge fixed

![Figure 2d](figures/fig2_d.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge simply supported and inner edge fixed, showing radius $r_o$ and uniform load $q$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0$

$$
M_{rb} = -qa^2 \frac{C_3L_{17} - C_9L_{11}}{C_2C_9 - C_3C_8}
$$

$$
Q_b = qa \frac{C_2L_{17} - C_8L_{11}}{C_2C_9 - C_3C_8}
$$

$$
\theta_a = M_{rb}\frac{a}{D}C_5 + Q_b\frac{b}{D}C_6 - \frac{qa^3}{D}L_{14}
$$

$$
Q_a = Q_b\frac{b}{a} - \frac{q}{2a}(a^2 - r_o^2)
$$

If $r_o = b$ (uniform load over entire plate),

| $b/a$ | 0.1 | 0.3 | 0.5 | 0.7 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $K_{y\max}$ | $-0.0040$ | $-0.0014$ | $-0.0004$ | $-0.00004$ |
| $K_{\theta_a}$ | $0.0147$ | $0.0070$ | $0.0026$ | $0.00056$ |
| $K_{M_b}$ | $-0.2459$ | $-0.0939$ | $-0.0393$ | $-0.01257$ |
| $K_{Q_b}$ | $2.1375$ | $0.7533$ | $0.4096$ | $0.21259$ |

## 2e. Outer edge fixed, inner edge free

![Figure 2e](figures/fig2_e.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge fixed and inner edge free, showing radius $r_o$ and uniform load $q$. -->

$M_{rb} = 0,\quad Q_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0$

$$
y_b = \frac{-qa^4}{D}\left(\frac{C_1L_{14}}{C_4} - L_{11}\right)
$$

$$
\theta_b = \frac{qa^3L_{14}}{DC_4}
$$

$$
M_{ra} = -qa^2\left(L_{17} - \frac{C_7}{C_4}L_{14}\right)
$$

$$
Q_a = \frac{-q}{2a}(a^2 - r_o^2)
$$

If $r_o = b$ (uniform load over entire plate),

| $b/a$ | 0.1 | 0.3 | 0.5 | 0.7 | 0.9 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $K_{y_b}$ | $-0.0166$ | $-0.0132$ | $-0.0053$ | $-0.0009$ | $-0.00001$ |
| $K_{\theta_b}$ | $0.0159$ | $0.0256$ | $0.0149$ | $0.0040$ | $0.00016$ |
| $K_{M_{ra}}$ | $-0.1246$ | $-0.1135$ | $-0.0800$ | $-0.0361$ | $-0.00470$ |
| $K_{M_b}$ | $0.1448$ | $0.0778$ | $0.0271$ | $0.0052$ | $0.00016$ |

## 2f. Outer edge fixed, inner edge guided

![Figure 2f](figures/fig2_f.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge fixed and inner edge guided, showing radius $r_o$ and uniform load $q$. -->

$\theta_b = 0,\quad Q_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0$

$$
y_b = \frac{-qa^4}{D}\left(\frac{C_2L_{14}}{C_5} - L_{11}\right)
$$

$$
M_{rb} = \frac{qa^2L_{14}}{C_5}
$$

$$
M_{ra} = -qa^2\left(L_{17} - \frac{C_8}{C_5}L_{14}\right)
$$

$$
Q_a = \frac{-q}{2a}(a^2 - r_o^2)
$$

If $r_o = b$ (uniform load over entire plate),
