| 1a. Uniform radial force $Q_o$ at the edge

![Figure 1a](figures/fig1a.png)
<!-- Description: Shell edge diagram showing uniform radial forces $Q_o$ applied at both sides of the edge, with angle $\phi$ indicated from the vertical centerline. --> | $C=\dfrac{Q_o(\sin \phi)^{3/2}\sqrt{1+K_1^2}}{K_1}$ and $\zeta=\tan^{-1}(-K_1)$ where $-\dfrac{\pi}{2}<\zeta<\dfrac{\pi}{2}$

Max value of $M_1$ occurs at $\omega=\pi/4\beta$

At the edge where $\omega=0$,

$V_1=Q_o\sin \phi,\qquad M_1=0,\qquad \sigma'_1=0,\qquad \sigma_1=\dfrac{Q_o\cos \phi}{t}$

$\sigma_2=\dfrac{Q_o\beta\sin \phi}{2t}\left(\dfrac{2}{K_1}+K_1+K_2\right)= (\sigma_2)_{\max}$

$M_2=\dfrac{Q_ot^2\beta^2\cos \phi}{6K_1R_2},\qquad \sigma'_2=\dfrac{-Q_oB^2\cos \phi}{K_1R_2}$

$\psi=\dfrac{Q_o2\beta^3\sin \phi}{EtK_1},\qquad \Delta R=\dfrac{Q_oR_2\beta\sin^2\phi}{EtK_1}(1+K_1K_2)$

(Refs. 14 and 42) |
| 1b. Uniform edge moment $M_o$

![Figure 1b](figures/fig1b.png)
<!-- Description: Shell edge diagram showing equal applied edge moments $M_o$ on both sides of the edge, with angle $\phi$ indicated from the vertical centerline. --> | $C=\dfrac{M_o2\beta\sqrt{\sin \phi}}{R_2K_1},\qquad \zeta=0$

At the edge where $\omega=0$,

$V_1=0,\qquad \sigma_1=0,\qquad M_1=M_o,\qquad \sigma'_1=\dfrac{-6M_o}{t^2}$

$\sigma_2=\dfrac{M_o2\beta^2}{R_2K_1t},\qquad M_2=\dfrac{M_o}{2\nu K_1}\left[(1+\nu^2)(K_1+K_2)-2K_2\right],\qquad \sigma'_2=\dfrac{-6M_2}{t^2}$

$\psi=\dfrac{M_o4\beta^3}{EtR_2K_1},\qquad \Delta R=\dfrac{M_o2\beta^2\sin \phi}{EtK_1}$

(Refs. 14 and 42) |
| 1c. Radial displacement $\Delta_o$; no edge rotation

![Figure 1c](figures/fig1c.png)
<!-- Description: Shell edge diagram showing prescribed radial displacement $\Delta_o$ at the edge with no edge rotation, and angle $\phi$ indicated from the vertical centerline. --> | $C=\dfrac{-\Delta_oEt}{R_2\beta K_2\sqrt{\sin \phi}},\qquad \zeta=\dfrac{\pi}{2}=90^\circ$

At the edge where $\omega=0$,

$V_1=\dfrac{\Delta_oEt}{R_2\beta K_2\sin \phi},\qquad \sigma_1=\dfrac{\Delta_oE\cos \phi}{R_2\beta K_2\sin^2\phi}$

Resultant radial edge force $=\dfrac{\Delta_oEt}{R_2\beta K_2\sin^2\phi}$

$M_1=\dfrac{-\Delta_oEt}{2\beta^2K_2\sin \phi},\qquad \sigma'_1=\dfrac{3\Delta_oE}{t\beta^2K_2\sin \phi}$

$\sigma_2=\dfrac{\Delta_oE(K_1+K_2)}{2R_2K_2\sin \phi},\qquad M_2=\dfrac{-\Delta_oEt\nu}{2\beta^2K_2\sin \phi},\qquad \sigma'_2=\dfrac{3\Delta_oE\nu}{t\beta^2K_2\sin \phi}$

$\psi=0,\qquad \Delta R=\Delta_o$ |
