**2h. Right end supported and slope-guided, left end simply supported**

![Figure 2h](figures/fig2_h.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with left end simply supported and right end supported and slope-guided, showing reactions $V_A$, $V_B$, end moment $M_B$, and applied moment $M_o$. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0
$$

$$
T_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = -\frac{M_o}{R}\frac{\sin(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{M_oR}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a1}\sin\phi + C_6\sin(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi} - \frac{C_3\sin\phi\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2} - C_{a4}\right]
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{M_oR}{EI}\left[\frac{C_{a4}\sin\phi - C_{a1}\cos\phi}{1-\cos\phi} + \frac{(C_3\cos\phi - C_6\sin\phi)\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}\right]
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
M_B = V_AR\sin\phi + M_o\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\psi_B = \psi_A\cos\phi - \Theta_A\sin\phi + \frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9 + \frac{M_oR}{EI}C_{a7}
$$

**2i. Right end supported and roll-guided, left end supported and roll-guided**

![Figure 2i](figures/fig2_i.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with both ends supported and roll-guided, showing reactions $V_A$, $T_A$, $V_B$, $T_B$, and applied moment $M_o$. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0
$$

$$
M_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = -\frac{M_o}{R}\frac{(C_{a1}+C_{a7})\sin^2\phi + (C_2+C_8)\cos(\phi-\theta)\sin\phi}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin^2\phi}
$$

$$
T_A = -M_o\frac{(C_{a1}+C_{a7})\sin^2\phi - (C_3+C_9)\cos(\phi-\theta)\sin\phi}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin^2\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{M_oR}{EI}\frac{[C_{a1}(C_8+C_9)-C_{a7}(C_2+C_3)]\sin\phi + (C_2C_9-C_3C_8)\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin^2\phi}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
T_B = V_AR(1-\cos\phi) + T_A\cos\phi + M_o\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\Theta_B = \Theta_A\cos\phi + \frac{T_A R}{EI}C_5 + \frac{V_A R^2}{EI}C_6 + \frac{M_o R}{EI}C_{a4}
$$
