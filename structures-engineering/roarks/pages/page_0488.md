## 7d. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge fixed

$$

$$
M_{rb} = \frac{-y_0 D C_9}{a^2 (C_2 C_9 - C_3 C_8)}

$$
\theta_a = \frac{y_0}{a}\frac{C_6 C_8 - C_3 C_9}{C_2 C_9 - C_3 C_8}

$$
Q_a = Q_b \frac{b}{a}
