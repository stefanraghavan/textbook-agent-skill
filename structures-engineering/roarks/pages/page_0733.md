# TABLE 15.1 Formulas for elastic stability of bars, rings, and beams *(Continued)*

| Case | Description | Formula and notes |
|---|---|---|
| 14. | Straight uniform I-beam under pure bending; $d =$ depth center to center of flange; ends constrained against twisting  | ![Figure 15.1: Case 14 diagram](figures/fig15_1.png) <!-- Description: Straight I-beam of length $l$ under equal end moments $M$; cross-section shown with vertical axis $y$ and flange-center spacing $d$. --> $$M'=\frac{\pi}{l}\sqrt{EI_yKG}\sqrt{1+\pi^2\frac{I_fEd^2}{2KGl^2}}$$ where $I_y$ is the moment of inertia of the cross section about its vertical axis of symmetry, $I_f$ is the moment of inertia of one flange about this axis, and $KG$ is the torsional rigidity of the section (see Table 10.1, case 26)  (For tapered I-beams, see Ref. 47.)  (Refs. 1, 3) |
| 15. | Straight uniform cantilever beam of I-section under end load applied at centroid of cross section; $d =$ depth center to center of flanges | ![Figure 15.2: Case 15 diagram](figures/fig15_2.png) <!-- Description: Cantilever I-beam of length $l$ fixed at left and loaded downward by end load $P$ at the free end; cross-section shown with vertical axis $y$ and flange-center spacing $d$. --> $$P'=\frac{m\sqrt{EI_yKG}}{l^2}$$ where $m$ is approximately equal to $4.01+11.7\sqrt{\frac{I_fEd^2}{2KGl^2}}$ and $I_y$, $I_f$, and $KG$ have the same significance as in case 14  (For unsymmetric I-beams, see Refs. 52 and 56; for tapered I-beams, see Ref. 55.)  (Refs. 1, 3) |
| 16. | Straight uniform I-beam loaded at centroid of middle section; ends simply supported and constrained against twisting | ![Figure 15.3: Case 16 diagram](figures/fig15_3.png) <!-- Description: Simply supported straight I-beam of length $l$ with a central downward load $P$ at midspan; cross-section shown with vertical axis $y$ and flange-center spacing $d$. --> $$P'=\frac{m\sqrt{EI_yKG}}{l^2}$$ where $m$ is approximately equal to $16.93+45\left(\frac{I_fEd^2}{2KGl^2}\right)^{0.8}$ and $I_y$, $I_f$, and $KG$ have same significance as in case 14.  (For unsymmetric I-beams, see Refs. 52 and 56; for tapered I-beams, see Ref. 55.)  (Refs. 1, 3) |
