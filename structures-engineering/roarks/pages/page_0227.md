Uniformly distributed load from $a$ to $b$  ![Figure 2: Uniformly distributed load from a to b](figures/fig2.png) <!-- Description: A semi-infinite beam on an elastic foundation with a uniformly distributed load of intensity w applied between distances a and b from the left end. --> | $R_A = 0$  $M_A = 0$  $$\theta_A = \frac{-w}{EI\beta^3}(B_3 - A_3)$$ $$y_A = \frac{-w}{2EI\beta^4}(B_2 - A_2)$$ | $R_A = 0$  $\theta_A = 0$  $$M_A = \frac{-w}{\beta^2}(B_3 - A_3)$$ $$y_A = \frac{w}{2EI\beta^4}(B_1 - A_1)$$ | $M_A = 0$  $y_A = 0$  $$R_A = \frac{w}{\beta}(B_2 - A_2)$$ $$\theta_A = \frac{w}{2EI\beta^3}(B_4 - A_4)$$ | $\theta_A = 0$  $y_A = 0$  $$R_A = \frac{-2w}{\beta}(B_1 - A_1)$$ $$M_A = \frac{w}{\beta^2}(B_4 - A_4)$$ | $$LT_V = \frac{-w}{2\beta}(F_{a2} - F_{b2})$$ $$LT_M = \frac{-w}{2\beta^2}(F_{a3} - F_{b3})$$ $$LT_\theta = \frac{-w}{4EI\beta^3}(F_{a4} - F_{b4})$$ $$LT_y = \frac{-w}{4EI\beta^4}(F_{a5} - F_{b5})$$ |
| 3. Uniform increasing load from $a$ to $b$  ![Figure 3: Uniform increasing load from a to b](figures/fig3.png) <!-- Description: A semi-infinite beam on an elastic foundation with a triangular distributed load increasing from zero at a to w at b. --> | $R_A = 0$  $M_A = 0$  $$\theta_A = \frac{w}{2EI\beta^4}\left(\frac{B_4 - A_4}{b-a} - 2\beta B_3\right)$$ $$y_A = \frac{w}{2EI\beta^5}\left(\frac{B_3 - A_3}{b-a} - \beta B_2\right)$$ | $R_A = 0$  $\theta_A = 0$  $$M_A = \frac{w}{2\beta^3}\left(\frac{B_4 - A_4}{b-a} - 2\beta B_3\right)$$ $$y_A = \frac{w}{4EI\beta^5}\left(\frac{B_2 - A_2}{b-a} - 2\beta B_1\right)$$ | $M_A = 0$  $y_A = 0$  $$R_A = \frac{-w}{\beta^2}\left(\frac{B_3 - A_3}{b-a} - \beta B_2\right)$$ $$\theta_A = \frac{w}{2EI\beta^4}\left(\frac{B_1 - A_1}{b-a} + \beta B_4\right)$$ | $\theta_A = 0$  $y_A = 0$  $$R_A = \frac{w}{\beta^2}\left(\frac{B_2 - A_2}{b-a} - 2\beta B_1\right)$$ $$M_A = \frac{w}{\beta^3}\left(\frac{B_1 - A_1}{b-a} + \beta B_4\right)$$ | $$LT_V = \frac{-w}{2\beta^2}\left(\frac{F_{a3} - F_{b3}}{b-a} - \beta F_{b2}\right)$$ $$LT_M = \frac{-w}{4\beta^3}\left(\frac{F_{a4} - F_{b4}}{b-a} - 2\beta F_{b3}\right)$$ $$LT_\theta = \frac{-w}{4EI\beta^4}\left(\frac{F_{a5} - F_{b5}}{b-a} - \beta F_{b4}\right)$$ $$LT_y = \frac{-w}{8EI\beta^5}\left(\frac{F_{a6} - F_{b6}}{b-a} - 2\beta F_{b5}\right)$$ |
| 4. Concentrated intermediate moment (if $\beta a > 3$, see case 11)  ![Figure 4: Concentrated intermediate moment](figures/fig4.png) <!-- Description: A semi-infinite beam on an elastic foundation with a concentrated moment M0 applied at distance a from the left end. --> | $R_A = 0$  $M_A = 0$  $$\theta_A = \frac{-2M_o}{EI\beta}A_1$$ $$y_A = \frac{M_o}{EI\beta^2}A_4$$  (if $a = 0$, see case 9) | $R_A = 0$  $\theta_A = 0$  $$M_A = -2M_oA_1$$ $$y_A = \frac{-M_o}{EI\beta^2}A_3$$ | $M_A = 0$  $y_A = 0$  $$R_A = -2M_o\beta A_4$$ $$\theta_A = \frac{M_o}{EI\beta}A_2$$ | $\theta_A = 0$  $y_A = 0$  $$R_A = 4M_o\beta A_3$$ $$M_A = 2M_oA_2$$ | $$LT_V = -M_o\beta F_{a4}$$ $$LT_M = M_oF_{a1}$$ $$LT_\theta = \frac{M_o}{2EI\beta}F_{a2}$$ $$LT_y = \frac{M_o}{2EI\beta^2}F_{a3}$$ |
