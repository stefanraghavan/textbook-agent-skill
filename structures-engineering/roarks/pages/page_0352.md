# TABLE 9.3 Reaction and deformation formulas for circular arches *(Continued)*

**5s.** Linear temperature differential through the thickness $t$ for that span to the right of point $Q$

![Figure 9.3-5s: Linear temperature differential through the thickness for the span to the right of point Q](figures/fig9_3_5s.png)
<!-- Description: A circular arch segment with point Q marked, angle phi shown, and temperatures T1 and T2 indicated through the thickness. -->

$$
LF_H = (T_1 - T_2)\frac{\gamma EI}{Rt}(n + s - \theta c - \phi c)
$$

$$
LF_V = (T_1 - T_2)\frac{\gamma EI}{Rt}(m - c + \theta s + \phi s)
$$

$$
LF_M = (T_1 - T_2)\frac{\gamma EI}{Rt}(\theta + \phi)
$$

*Note:* The temperature at the centroidal axis is the initial unloaded temperature

**6.** Left end pinned, right end fixed

![Figure 9.3-6: Left end pinned, right end fixed](figures/fig9_3_6.png)
<!-- Description: A circular arch with the left end pinned showing reactions H_A and V_A, and the right end fixed. -->

Since $\delta_{HA} = 0$, $\delta_{VA} = 0$, and $M_A = 0$,

$$
H_A = \frac{B_{VV}LF_H - B_{HV}LF_V}{B_{HH}B_{VV} - B_{HV}^2}
$$

$$
V_A = \frac{B_{HH}LF_V - B_{HV}LF_H}{B_{HH}B_{VV} - B_{HV}^2}
$$

$$
\psi_A = \frac{R^2}{EI}(B_{MH}H_A + B_{MV}V_A - LF_M)
$$

Use load terms given above for cases 5a–5s

**7.** Left end guided in horizontal direction, right end fixed

![Figure 9.3-7: Left end guided in horizontal direction, right end fixed](figures/fig9_3_7.png)
<!-- Description: A circular arch with the left end guided horizontally showing moment M_A and vertical reaction V_A, and the right end fixed. -->

Since $\delta_{VA} = 0$, $\psi_A = 0$, and $H_A = 0$,

$$
V_A = \frac{B_{MM}LF_V - B_{MV}LF_M}{B_{VV}B_{MM} - B_{MV}^2}
$$

$$
\frac{M_A}{R} = \frac{B_{VV}LF_M - B_{MV}LF_V}{B_{VV}B_{MM} - B_{MV}^2}
$$

$$
\delta_{HA} = \frac{R^3}{EI}\left(B_{VH}V_A + B_{HM}\frac{M_A}{R} - LF_H\right)
$$

Use load terms given above for cases 5a–5s

---

**8.** Left end guided in vertical direction, right end fixed

![Figure 9.3-8: Left end guided in vertical direction, right end fixed](figures/fig9_3_8.png)
<!-- Description: A circular arch with the left end guided vertically showing moment M_A and horizontal reaction H_A, and the right end fixed. -->

Since $\delta_{HA} = 0$, $\psi_A = 0$, and $V_A = 0$,

$$
H_A = \frac{B_{MM}LF_H - B_{HM}LF_M}{B_{HH}B_{MM} - B_{HM}^2}
$$

$$
\frac{M_A}{R} = \frac{B_{HH}LF_M - B_{HM}LF_H}{B_{HH}B_{MM} - B_{HM}^2}
$$

$$
\delta_{VA} = \frac{R^3}{EI}\left(B_{VH}H_A + B_{VM}\frac{M_A}{R} - LF_V\right)
$$

Use load terms given above for cases 5a–5s
