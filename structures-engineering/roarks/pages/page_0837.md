For balanced-symmetric laminates, it is also possible to calculate effective engineering properties. They are

$$
E_x = \frac{1}{h}\left[A_{11} - \frac{A_{12}^2}{A_{22}}\right]
$$

$$
E_y = \frac{1}{h}\left[A_{22} - \frac{A_{12}^2}{A_{11}}\right]
$$

$$
\nu_{xy} = \frac{A_{12}}{A_{22}}
$$

$$
G_{xy} = \frac{A_{66}}{h}
\qquad \text{(C.8)}
$$

where $h$ is the total laminate thickness.
