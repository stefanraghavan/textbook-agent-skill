# TABLE 11.2 Formulas for flat circular plates of constant thickness *(Continued)*

Case 6. Annular plate with an externally applied change in slope $\theta_0$ on an annulus with a radius $r_o$

![Figure 11.2: Case 6 annular plate with externally applied change in slope](figures/fig11_2.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of an annular plate showing dimensions a, b, and r_o, with labeled quantities y_b, M_rb, Q_b at the inner region and M_ra, Q_a, theta_0 at the outer annulus. -->

General expressions for deformations, moments, and shears:

$$
y=y_b+\theta_b rF_1+M_{rb}\frac{r^2}{D}F_2+Q_b\frac{r^3}{D}F_3+\theta_o rG_1
$$

$$
\theta=\theta_bF_4+M_{rb}\frac{r}{D}F_5+Q_b\frac{r^2}{D}F_6+\theta_oG_4
$$

$$
M_r=\theta_b\frac{D}{r}F_7+M_{rb}F_8+Q_brF_9+\frac{\theta_oD}{r}G_7
$$

$$
M_t=\frac{\theta D(1-\nu^2)}{r}+\nu M_r
$$

$$
Q=Q_b\frac{b}{r}
$$

For the numerical data given below, $\nu=0.3$, and all values given for $K_{Mto}$ are found just outside $r_o$

$$
y=K_y\theta_0a,\qquad \theta=K_\theta\theta_o,\qquad M=K_M\theta_o\frac{D}{a},\qquad Q=K_Q\theta_o\frac{D}{a^2}
$$
