**3i. Right end supported and roll-guided, left end supported and roll-guided.**

![Figure 9.4-3i: Right end supported and roll-guided, left end supported and roll-guided](figures/fig9_4_3i.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam diagram with end forces and moments labeled $V_A$, $V_B$, $T_A$, $T_B$, and applied torque $T_0$. Boundary conditions shown at A and B. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0
$$

$$
M_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = 0
$$

$$
T_A = -T_0 \frac{(C_{a2}+C_{a8})\sin^2\phi + (C_3+C_9)\sin(\phi-\theta)\sin\phi}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin^2\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{T_0R}{EI}\frac{[C_{a2}(C_8+C_9)-C_{a8}(C_2+C_3)]\sin\phi-(C_2C_9-C_3C_8)\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin^2\phi}
$$

$$
V_B = 0
$$

$$
T_B = V_A R(1-\cos\phi) + T_A\cos\phi + T_0\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\Theta_B = \Theta_A\cos\phi + \frac{T_AR}{EI}C_5 + \frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_6 + \frac{T_0R}{EI}C_{a5}
$$

**3j. Right end supported and roll-guided, left end simply supported**

![Figure 9.4-3j: Right end supported and roll-guided, left end simply supported](figures/fig9_4_3j.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam diagram with end forces and moments labeled $V_A$, $V_B$, $T_B$, and applied torque $T_0$. Boundary conditions shown for simply supported left end and supported, roll-guided right end. -->

$$
M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0
$$

$$
M_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0
$$

$$
V_A = \frac{T_0\sin(\phi-\theta)}{R\sin\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{T_0R}{EI}\left\{\frac{[C_{a2}\cos\phi - C_{a8}(1-\cos\phi)]}{\sin\phi} + [C_3\cos\phi - C_9(1-\cos\phi)]\frac{\sin(\phi-\theta)}{\sin^2\phi}\right\}
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{T_0R}{EI}\left[C_{a2}+C_{a8}+(C_3+C_9)\frac{\sin(\phi-\theta)}{\sin\phi}\right]
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
T_B = V_A R(1-\cos\phi) + T_0\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\Theta_B = \Theta_A\cos\phi + \psi_A\sin\phi + \frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_6 + \frac{T_0R}{EI}C_{a5}
$$
