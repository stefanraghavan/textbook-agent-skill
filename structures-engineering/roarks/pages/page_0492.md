Cases 9 to 15. Solid circular plate under the several indicated loadings

![Figure 11.2-cases9to15: Solid circular plate general expressions diagram](figures/fig11_2_cases9_15.png)
<!-- Description: Diagram of a solid circular plate showing center deflection $y_c$, deflection $y$ at radius $r$, slope $\theta$, edge moment $M_{ra}$, edge shear $Q_a$, radius $a$, and center moment notation. -->

General expressions for deformations, moments, and shears:

$$
y=y_c+\frac{M_cr^2}{2D(1+\nu)}+LT_y
\qquad
\text{(Note: }y_c\text{ is the center deflection)}
$$

$$
\theta=\frac{M_cr}{D(1+\nu)}+LT_\theta
\qquad
\text{(Note: }M_c\text{ is the moment at the center)}
$$

$$
M_r=M_c+LT_M
$$

$$
M_t=\frac{\theta D(1-\nu^2)}{r}+\nu M_r
\qquad
\text{(Note: For }r<r_o,\ M_t=M_r=M_c\text{)}
$$

$$
Q_r=LT_Q
$$

For the numerical data given below, $\nu=0.3$
\qquad
(Note: $\ln =$ natural logarithm)
