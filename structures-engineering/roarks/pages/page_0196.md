<!-- Description: Beam with partial distributed load varying from $w_a$ to $w_l$ over length $(l-a)$, showing reactions $R_A$, $R_B$, end moments $M_A$, $M_B$, slopes $\theta_A$, $\theta_B$, deflections $y_A$, and span dimensions $a$ and $l$. -->

Transverse shear
$$
V=R_A-w_a\langle x-a\rangle-\frac{w_l-w_a}{2(l-a)}\langle x-a\rangle^2
$$

Bending moment
$$
M=M_A+R_Ax-\frac{w_a}{2}\langle x-a\rangle^2-\frac{w_l-w_a}{6(l-a)}\langle x-a\rangle^3
$$

Slope
$$
\theta=\theta_A+\frac{M_Ax}{EI}+\frac{R_Ax^2}{2EI}-\frac{w_a}{6EI}\langle x-a\rangle^3-\frac{w_l-w_a}{24EI(l-a)}\langle x-a\rangle^4
$$

Deflection
$$
y=y_A+\theta_Ax+\theta_lx+\frac{M_Ax^2}{2EI}+\frac{R_Ax^3}{6EI}-\frac{w_a}{24EI}\langle x-a\rangle^4-\frac{(w_l-w_a)}{120EI(l-a)}\langle x-a\rangle^5
$$
