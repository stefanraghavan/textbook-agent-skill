## 4b. Uniform edge moment $M_A$

![Figure 4b: Uniform edge moment on conical shell](figures/fig4b.png)
<!-- Description: A conical shell with a uniform edge moment applied around the large end, indicated by curved arrows and labeled $M_A$. -->

$$
N_1=M_A\left(\frac{k_A}{k}\right)^{5/2}\frac{2\sqrt{2}\beta\rho}{tk_A}\frac{e^{-\mu}}{C_1}(F_1\cos\mu-F_2\sin\mu)
$$

$$
N_2=M_A\left(\frac{k_A}{k}\right)^{3/2}\frac{\beta}{t}\frac{e^{-\mu}}{C_1}(F_7\cos\mu-F_8\sin\mu)
$$

$$
M_1=M_A\left(\frac{k_A}{k}\right)^{3/2}\frac{e^{-\mu}}{C_1}\left[\left(F_8+\frac{2\sqrt{2}\nu}{k}F_2\right)\cos\mu+\left(F_7+\frac{2\sqrt{2}\nu}{k}F_1\right)\sin\mu\right]
$$

$$
M_2=M_A\left(\frac{k_A}{k}\right)^{3/2}\nu\frac{e^{-\mu}}{C_1}\left[\left(F_8+\frac{2\sqrt{2}}{\nu k}F_2\right)\cos\mu+\left(F_7+\frac{2\sqrt{2}}{\nu k}F_1\right)\sin\mu\right]
$$

$$
\Delta R=M_A\left(\frac{k_A}{k}\right)^{3/2}\frac{\beta R}{Et^2}\frac{e^{-\mu}}{C_1}\left[\left(F_7-\frac{2\sqrt{2}\nu}{k}F_1\right)\cos\mu-\left(F_8-\frac{2\sqrt{2}\nu}{k}F_2\right)\sin\mu\right]
$$

$$
\psi=M_A\left(\frac{k_A}{k}\right)^{1/2}\frac{2\sqrt{2}\beta^2R_A}{Et^3k_A\sin\alpha}\frac{e^{-\mu}}{C_1}(F_2\cos\mu+F_1\sin\mu)
$$

At the loaded end where $R=R_A$:

$$
N_{1A}=0,\qquad N_{2A}=M_A\frac{\beta}{tC_1}F_{7A}
$$

$$
M_{1A}=M_A,\qquad M_{2A}=M_A\left[\nu+\frac{2\sqrt{2}(1-\nu^2)}{k_AC_1}F_{2A}\right]
$$

$$
\Delta R_A=M_A\frac{\beta R_A}{Et^2C_1}F_{7A},\qquad
\psi_A=M_A\frac{2\sqrt{2}\beta^2R_A}{Et^3k_AC_1\sin\alpha}F_{2A}
$$

## 5. Short conical shells. Expressions are accurate if $R/(t\cos\alpha)>10$ and $|k|>5$ everywhere in the cone

![Figure 5: Short conical shell geometry and stress resultants](figures/fig5.png)
<!-- Description: A short conical shell showing large-end radius $R_A$, smaller radius $R_B$, cone angle $\alpha$, and local stress resultants $N_1$, $N_2$, $M_1$, and shear $V_1$. -->

$$
k=\frac{2}{\sin\alpha}\left[\frac{12(1-\nu^2)R^2}{t^2\sec^2\alpha}\right]^{1/4},
\qquad
\mu_D=\left|\frac{k_A-k_B}{\sqrt{2}}\right|,
\qquad
\beta=[12(1-\nu^2)]^{1/2},
$$

$$
\sigma_1=\frac{N_1}{t},\qquad
\sigma_2=\frac{N_2}{t}
$$

$$
\sigma_1'=\frac{-6M_1}{t^2},\qquad
\sigma_2'=\frac{-6M_2}{t^2}
$$

$$
V_1=\frac{N_1}{\tan\alpha}
$$

## 5a. Uniform radial force $Q_A$ at the large end

![Figure 5a: Uniform radial force at the large end](figures/fig5a.png)
<!-- Description: A short conical shell with uniform radial force $Q_A$ applied around the large end and radial displacement $h$ indicated. -->

$$
N_1=Q_A\sin\alpha\,K_{N1},
\qquad
N_2=Q_A\sin\alpha\,\frac{k_A}{\sqrt{2}}K_{N2}
$$

$$
M_1=Q_A\sin\alpha\,\frac{k_At}{\sqrt{2}\beta}K_{M1},
\qquad
M_2=Q_A\nu\sin\alpha\,\frac{k_At}{\sqrt{2}\beta}K_{M2},
\qquad
\Delta h=\frac{Q_AR_A}{Et}K_{\Delta h1}-\frac{Q_AR_A\sin^2\alpha}{Et\cos\alpha}\frac{k_A}{\sqrt{2}}K_{\Delta h2}
$$

$$
\Delta R=\frac{Q_AR_A\sin\alpha}{Et}\frac{k_A}{\sqrt{2}}K_{\Delta R},
\qquad
\psi=\frac{Q_AR_A\beta}{Et^2}K_{\psi}
$$
