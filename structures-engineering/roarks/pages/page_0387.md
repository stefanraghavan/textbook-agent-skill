## 10.2 Bars of Noncircular Uniform Section under Pure Torsion

The formulas of this section are based on the same assumptions as those of Sec. 10.1 except that the cross section of the bar is not circular. It is important to note that the condition of loading implies that the end sections of the bar are free to warp, there being no constraining forces to hold them in their respective planes.

**Behavior.** The bar twists, each section rotating about its torsional center. Sections do not remain plane, but warp, and some radial lines through the torsional center do not remain straight. The distribution of shear stress on the section is not necessarily linear, and the direction of the shear stress is not necessarily normal to a radius.
