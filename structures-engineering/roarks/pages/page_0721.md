**Corrugated tubes or bellows.** An instability can develop when a corrugated tube or bellows is subjected to an internal pressure with the ends partially or totally restrained against axial displacement. (This instability can also occur in very long cylindrical vessels under similar restraints.) For a discussion and an example of this effect, see Sec. 13.5.
