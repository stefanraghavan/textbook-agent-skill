## 7a. Uniform over entire plate

![Figure 7a: Uniform over entire plate](figures/fig7a.png)
<!-- Description: Vertical plate strip fixed at the bottom and subjected to a uniform lateral load over its full height. -->

(At center of fixed edge) \quad $\sigma = \dfrac{-\beta_1 q b^2}{t^2}$ and $R = \gamma_1 q b$

(At center of free edge) \quad $\sigma = \dfrac{\beta_2 q b^2}{t^2}$

(At end of free edge) \quad $R = \gamma_2 q b$
