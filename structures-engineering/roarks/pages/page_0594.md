## 13.7 Pipe on Supports at Intervals

For a pipe or cylindrical tank supported at intervals on saddles or pedestals and filled or partly filled with liquid, the stress analysis is difficult and the results are rendered uncertain by doubtful boundary conditions. Certain conclusions arrived at from a study of tests (Refs. 11 and 12) may be helpful in guiding design: See also Ref. 75.
