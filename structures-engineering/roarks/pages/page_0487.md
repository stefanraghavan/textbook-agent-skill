Case 7. Annular plate with an externally applied vertical deformation $y_o$ at a radius $r_o$

![Figure 11.2: Annular plate with an externally applied vertical deformation at radius $r_o$](figures/fig11_2.png)

General expressions for deformations, moments, and shears:

$$
y = y_b + \theta_b r F_1 + M_{rb}\frac{r^2}{D}F_2 + Q_b\frac{r^3}{D}F_3 + y_o(r-r_o)^0
$$

$$
\theta = \theta_b F_4 + M_{rb}\frac{r}{D}F_5 + Q_b\frac{r^2}{D}F_6
$$

$$
M_r = \theta_b\frac{D}{r}F_7 + M_{rb}F_8 + Q_brF_9
$$

$$
M_t = \frac{\theta D(1-\nu^2)}{r} + \nu M_r
$$

$$
Q = Q_b\frac{b}{r}
$$

For the numerical data given below, $\nu = 0.3$

$$
y = K_y y_o, \qquad \theta = K_\theta \frac{y_o}{a}, \qquad M = K_M y_o \frac{D}{a^2}, \qquad Q = K_Q y_o \frac{D}{a^3}
$$
