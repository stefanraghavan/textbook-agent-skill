## EXAMPLE

A three-element rectangular rosette strain gage is installed on a steel specimen. For a particular state of loading of the structure the strain gage readings are$^\dagger$

$$
\varepsilon_A = 200\ \mu,\qquad \varepsilon_B = 900\ \mu,\qquad \varepsilon_C = 1000\ \mu
$$

$\dagger$ The strain gage readings are typically corrected due to the effect of transverse strains on each gage. This will be discussed shortly.
