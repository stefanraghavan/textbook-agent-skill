# TABLE 13.4 Formulas for discontinuity stresses and deformations at the junctions of shells and plates *(Continued)*

**2d. Rotation around the axis of symmetry at $\omega$ rad/s**

![Figure 13.4: Rotation around the axis of symmetry at $\omega$ rad/s](figures/fig13_4.png)
<!-- Description: Sketch of a cylinder joined to a cone, rotating about its axis of symmetry with angular velocity $\omega$. -->

*Note: $\delta =$ mass/unit volume*

$$
LT_{A1}=\frac{R_1^2}{t_1^2}
$$

$$
LT_{A2}=-\frac{\delta_2 R_2^3 E_1}{\delta_1 t_1^2 E_2 R_1}
$$

$$
LT_{AC}=0
$$

$$
LT_{B1}=0,\qquad LT_{B2}=-\frac{\delta_2 R_2^2 E_1\,(3+\nu_2)\tan\alpha_2}{\delta_1 E_2 t_1 R_1}
$$

$$
LT_{BC}=0
$$

At the junction of the cylinder and cone,

$$
V_1=\delta_1\omega^2 R_1 t_1^2 K_{V1},\qquad
M_1=\delta_1\omega^2 R_1 t_1^3 K_{M1}
$$

$$
N_1=0
$$

$$
\Delta R_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2 R_1 t_1^2}{E_1}\left(LT_{A1}-K_{V1}C_{AA1}-K_{M1}C_{AB1}\right)
$$

$$
\psi_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2 R_1 t_1}{E_1}\left(K_{V1}C_{AB1}+K_{M1}C_{BB1}\right)
$$

For $R_1=R_2,\ E_1=E_2,\ \nu_1=\nu_2=0.3,\ \delta_1=\delta_2$, and for $R/t>5$,

$$
\Delta R_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2 R_1^3}{E_1}K_{\Delta RA},\qquad
\psi_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2 R_1^2}{E_1}K_{\psi A},\qquad
\sigma_2=\delta_1\omega^2 R_1^2 K_{\sigma 2}
$$
