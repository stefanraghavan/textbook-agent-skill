**8. Solid trapezoidal section**

![Figure 12.1: Solid trapezoidal section](figures/fig12_1.png)

$$
q=\frac{b_1}{b},
\qquad
\bar{x}=\frac{d}{3}\frac{1+2q}{1+q},
\qquad
n=\frac{b}{8}\frac{1+q+q^2+q^3}{1+q+q^2}
$$

$$
m_1=\frac{d}{6}\frac{1+4q+q^2}{1+3q+2q^2},
\qquad
m_2=\frac{d}{6}\frac{1+4q+q^2}{2+3q+q^2},
\qquad
m_3=\frac{d}{12}\frac{1+3q-3q^2-q^3}{1+2q+2q^2+q^3}
$$

$$
A=\frac{d(b+b_1)}{2}
$$

$$
\sigma_{\max}=\frac{P}{A}\frac{3(1+q)}{3qx/d+(1-q)(x/d)^2}
$$

where $x$ satisfies

$$
\frac{e}{d}=\frac{2+q}{3(1+q)}-\frac{x}{2d}\frac{2q+(1-q)x/d}{3q+(1-q)x/d}
$$
