# TABLE 13.4 Formulas for discontinuity stresses and deformations at the junctions of shells and plates *(Continued)*

**6d. Rotation around the axis of symmetry at $\omega$ rad/s**

![Figure 13.4: Rotation around the axis of symmetry at ω rad/s](figures/fig13_4.png)

*Note: $\delta =$ mass/unit volume*

$$
LT_{A1}=\frac{R_1^2}{t_1^2}
$$

$$
LT_{A2}=-\frac{\delta_2 R_2^3 E_1 \sin^3 \phi_2}{\delta_1 R_1 E_2 t_1^2}
$$

$$
LT_{AC}=0
$$

$$
LT_{B1}=-\frac{R_1(3+\nu_1)\tan \alpha_1}{t_1}
$$

$$
LT_{B2}=-\frac{\delta_2 R_2^2 E_1(3+\nu_2)\sin \phi_2 \cos \phi_2}{\delta_1 R_1 t_1 E_2}
$$

$$
LT_{BC}=0
$$

At the junction of the cone and sphere,

$$
V_1=\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1 t_1^2 K_{V1}, \qquad M_1=\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1 t_1^3 K_{M1}
$$

$$
N_1=-V_1 \sin \alpha_1
$$

$$
\Delta R_A=\frac{\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1^2 t_1^2}{E_1}\left(LT_{A1}-K_{V1}C_{AA1}-K_{M1}C_{AB1}\right)
$$

$$
\psi_A=\frac{\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1 t_1}{E_1}\left(-LT_{B1}+K_{V1}C_{AB1}+K_{M1}C_{BB1}\right)
$$

For $E_1=E_2$, $\nu_1=\nu_2=0.3$, $t_1=t_2$, $\delta_1=\delta_2$, $R_2 \sin \phi_2=R_1$, and for $R_1/t_1 \cos \alpha_1 > 5$ and $R_2/t_2 > 5$.

$$
\Delta R_A=\frac{\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1^3}{E_1}K_{\Delta RA}, \qquad
\psi_A=\frac{\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1^2}{E_1}K_{\psi A}, \qquad
\sigma_2=\delta_1 \omega^2 R_1^2 K_{\sigma 2}
$$
