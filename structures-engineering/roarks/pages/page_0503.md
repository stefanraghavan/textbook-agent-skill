Simply supported edge

Free edges

Formulas valid for $b \ge 0.7a$

(At A)
$$
\sigma_t=\frac{6qcb}{t^2}\left(\frac{b}{c}-\frac{1}{3}\right)\left[c_1\left(1-\gamma_1^2\frac{c}{b}\right)+c_2\left(1-\gamma_2^2\frac{c}{b}\right)+\frac{c}{b}\right]K
$$

max stress occurs on inner edge over central $60^\circ$

(At B)
$$
y=\frac{-24qc^2b^2}{Et^3}\left(\frac{b}{c}-\frac{1}{3}\right)\left[c_1\cosh \gamma_1\alpha+c_2\cosh \gamma_2\alpha+\frac{c}{b}\right]
$$

max deflection occurs when $\alpha=\dfrac{\pi}{2}$

where
$$
c_1=\frac{1}{\left(\frac{b}{c}-\gamma_1^2\right)(\lambda-1)\cosh \frac{\gamma_1\pi}{2}}
\qquad
c_2=\frac{1}{\left(\frac{b}{c}-\gamma_2^2\right)\left(\frac{1}{\lambda}-1\right)\cosh \frac{\gamma_2\pi}{2}}
$$

$$
\gamma_1=\frac{\gamma}{\sqrt{2}}\sqrt{1+\sqrt{1-\frac{4b^2}{c^2\gamma^4}}}
\qquad
\gamma_2=\frac{\gamma}{\sqrt{2}}\sqrt{1-\sqrt{1-\frac{4b^2}{c^2\gamma^4}}}
$$

$$
\gamma=\sqrt{\frac{2b}{c}+4\left(1-\frac{0.625t}{2c}\right)\frac{G}{E}\left(1+\frac{b}{c}\right)^2},
\qquad
\lambda=\frac{\gamma_1\left(\frac{b}{c}-\gamma_1^2+\lambda_1\right)\left(\frac{b}{c}-\gamma_2^2\right)\tanh \frac{\gamma_1\pi}{2}}
{\gamma_2\left(\frac{b}{c}-\gamma_2^2+\lambda_1\right)\left(\frac{b}{c}-\gamma_1^2\right)\tanh \frac{\gamma_2\pi}{2}},
\qquad
\lambda_1=4\left(1-\frac{0.625t}{2c}\right)\frac{G}{E}\left(1+\frac{b}{c}\right)^2
$$

$K=$ function of $(b-c)/(b+c)$ and has values as follows:

| $(b-c)/(b+c)$ | 0.4 | 0.5 | 0.6 | 0.7 | 0.8 | 0.9 | 1.0 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $K$ | 1.58 | 1.44 | 1.32 | 1.22 | 1.13 | 1.06 | 1.0 |
