# TABLE 8.8 Shear, moment, slope, and deflection formulas for beams under simultaneous axial compression and transverse loading *(Continued)*

## 1f. Left end guided, right end simply supported

![Figure 8.8-1f: Left end guided, right end simply supported](figures/fig8_8_1f.png)
<!-- Description: Beam diagram with axial compressive load P, a concentrated load W located a distance a from the left end, left end guided, and right end simply supported. -->

$$
R_A = 0 \qquad \theta_A = 0
$$

$$
M_A = \frac{W}{k}\sin k(l-a)\,\frac{1}{\cos kl}
$$

$$
y_A = -\frac{W}{kP}\left[\frac{\sin k(l-a)}{\cos kl} - k(l-a)\right]
$$

$$
R_B = W \qquad M_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0
$$

$$
\theta_B = \frac{W}{P}\left(\frac{\cos ka}{\cos kl} - 1\right)
$$

Max $M = M_A$; max possible value $= \dfrac{W}{k}\tan kl$ when $a = 0$

Max $\theta = \theta_B$; max possible value $= \dfrac{W}{P}\dfrac{1-\cos kl}{\cos kl}$ when $a = 0$

Max $y = y_A$; max possible value $= -\dfrac{W}{kP}(\tan kl - kl)$ when $a = 0$

## 2. Axial compressive load plus distributed lateral load

![Figure 8.8-2: Axial compressive load plus distributed lateral load](figures/fig8_8_2.png)
<!-- Description: Beam with axial compressive load P, distributed lateral load varying from w_a at distance a to w_l at the right end over span l, with end reactions and end quantities R_A, M_A, y_A, theta_A at left and R_B, M_B, theta_B at right; axes X and Y shown. -->

Transverse shear
$$
V = R_A F_1 - M_A kF_2 - \theta_A P F_1 - \frac{w_a}{k}F_{a2} - \frac{w_l-w_a}{k^2(l-a)}F_{a3}
$$

Bending moment
$$
M = M_A F_1 + \frac{R_A}{k}F_2 - \frac{\theta_A P}{k}F_2 - \frac{w_a}{k^2}F_{a3} - \frac{w_l-w_a}{k^3(l-a)}F_{a4}
$$

Slope
$$
\theta = \theta_A F_1 + \frac{M_A k}{P}F_2 + \frac{R_A}{P}F_3 - \frac{w_a}{kP}F_{a4} - \frac{w_l-w_a}{k^2P(l-a)}F_{a5}
$$

Deflection
$$
y = y_A + \frac{\theta_A}{k}F_2 + \frac{M_A}{P}F_3 + \frac{R_A}{kP}F_4 - \frac{w_a}{k^2P}F_{a5} - \frac{w_l-w_a}{k^3P(l-a)}F_{a6}
$$
