**TABLE 12.1**  **Formulas for short prisms loaded eccentrically; stress reversal impossible**

**NOTATION:** $m$ and $n$ are dimensions of the kern which is shown shaded in each case; $x$ is the distance from the most stressed fiber to the neutral axis; and $A$ is the net area of the section. Formulas for $x$ and for maximum stress assume the prism to be subjected to longitudinal load $P$ acting outside the kern on one principal axis of the section and at a distance $e$ from the other principal axis
