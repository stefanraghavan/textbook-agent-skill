**8. Solid semicircle or semielipse, base outward**

![Figure 8: Solid semicircle or semielipse, base outward](figures/fig8.png)
<!-- Description: A semicircular or semielliptical cross section with flat base on top and curved side downward. Dimensions shown are width $b$, radii $R_x$ and $R$, and vertical distances $c_1$, $c$, and $d$. -->

*(Note: for a semicircle, $b/2 = d$)*

$$
R = R_x - c_1,\qquad \frac{d}{c} = \frac{3\pi}{3\pi - 4},\qquad \frac{c_1}{c} = \frac{4}{3\pi - 4}
$$

*(Note: $e/c$, $k_i$, and $k_o$ are independent of the width $b$)*

$$
\int_{\text{area}} \frac{dA}{r}
=
\frac{\pi R_x b}{2d}
+
b
-
\frac{b}{d}\sqrt{R_x^2-d^2}
\left(
\frac{\pi}{2}
+
\sin^{-1}\frac{d}{R_x}
\right)
$$

$$
\frac{e}{c}
=
\frac{R}{c}
-
\frac{(d/c)^2/2}{
\frac{R}{c}
+
\frac{10}{3\pi-4}
-
\sqrt{\left(\frac{R}{c}+\frac{c_1}{c}\right)^2-\left(\frac{d}{c}\right)^2}
\left(
1+\frac{2}{\pi}\sin^{-1}\frac{d/c}{R/c+c_1/c}
\right)
}
$$

$$
k_i=\frac{0.2109\,\left(1-e/c\right)}{\left(e/c\right)\left(R/c-1\right)}
$$

$$
k_o=\frac{0.2860\,e/c+0.7374}{\left(e/c\right)\left(R/c+0.7374\right)}
$$
