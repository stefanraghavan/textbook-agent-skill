<!-- Description: A circular ring with vertical centerline, point A at the top, symmetric angles $\theta$ on each side of the top, and applied moments $M_0$ on the left and right upper sides. -->

$LT_M = M_0(x-\theta)^0$

$LT_N = 0$

$LT_V = 0$

$$
M_A = -\frac{M_0}{\pi}\left(\pi-\theta-\frac{2sk_2}{k_1}\right)
$$

$$
M_C = \frac{M_0}{\pi}\left(\theta-\frac{2sk_2}{k_1}\right)
$$

$$
N_A = \frac{M_0}{R\pi}\left(\frac{2sk_2}{k_1}\right)
$$

$$
V_A = 0
$$

$$
\Delta D_H=
\begin{cases}
\dfrac{M_0R^2}{EI}\,k_2\left(\dfrac{2\theta}{\pi}-s\right) & \text{if } \theta \le \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\
\dfrac{M_0R^2}{EI}\,k_2\left(\dfrac{2\theta}{\pi}-2+s\right) & \text{if } \theta \ge \dfrac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\Delta D_V=\frac{M_0R^2}{EI}\,k_2\left(\frac{2\theta}{\pi}-1+c\right)
$$

$$
\Delta L=
\begin{cases}
-\dfrac{M_0R^2}{EI}\left[\dfrac{\theta}{2}-\dfrac{k_2(\theta+s)}{\pi}\right] & \text{if } \theta \le \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\
-\dfrac{M_0R^2}{EI}\left[\pi-\theta-\dfrac{k_2(\theta+s+\pi c)}{\pi}\right] & \text{if } \theta \ge \dfrac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\Delta L_W=-\frac{M_0R^2}{EI\pi}\left[(\pi-\theta)\theta s-k_2(s^3+\theta+\theta c)\right]
$$

$$
\Delta L_{WH}=\frac{M_0R^2}{EI\pi}\left[2\theta c(\pi-\theta)+2k_2s(2\theta-\pi-sc)\right]
$$

$$
\Delta \psi=\frac{M_0R}{EI\pi}\left[\theta(\pi-\theta)-\frac{2s^2k_2^2}{k_1}\right]
$$

$$
\text{Max }+M=\frac{M_0}{\pi}\left(\theta+\frac{2sck_2}{k_1}\right)\quad \text{at } x \text{ just greater than } \theta
$$

$$
\text{Max }-M=-\frac{M_0}{\pi}\left(\pi-\theta-\frac{2sck_2}{k_1}\right)\quad \text{at } x \text{ just less than } \theta
$$

If $\alpha=\beta=0$, $M=K_M M_0$, $N=K_N M_0/R$, $\Delta D=K_{\Delta D}M_0R^2/EI$,

$\Delta \psi=K_{\Delta \psi}M_0R/EI$, etc.
