# TABLE 8.5 Shear, moment, slope, and deflection formulas for finite-length beams on elastic foundations *(Continued)*

3. Partial uniformly increasing load

![Figure 8.5: Partial uniformly increasing load diagram](figures/fig8_5.png)
<!-- Description: A beam on an elastic foundation with left-end reactions and deformation labels $M_A$, $R_A$, $\theta_A$, and $y_A$, x-axis to the right, y-axis upward, beam length $l$, and a partial uniformly increasing load over the right portion beginning at distance $a$ from the left end and increasing to intensity $w$ at the right end. -->

Transverse shear
$$
V = R_A F_1 - y_A 2EI\beta^3 F_2 - \theta_A 2EI\beta^2 F_3 - M_A \beta F_4 - \frac{wF_{a3}}{2\beta^2(l-a)}
$$

Bending moment
$$
M = M_A F_1 + \frac{R_A}{2\beta}F_2 - y_A 2EI\beta^2 F_3 - \theta_A EI\beta F_4 - \frac{wF_{a4}}{4\beta^3(l-a)}
$$

Slope
$$
\theta = \theta_A F_1 + \frac{M_A}{2EI\beta}F_2 + \frac{R_A}{2EI\beta^2}F_3 - y_A \beta F_4 - \frac{wF_{a5}}{4EI\beta^4(l-a)}
$$

Deflection
$$
y = y_A F_1 + \frac{\theta_A}{2\beta}F_2 + \frac{M_A}{2EI\beta^2}F_3 + \frac{R_A}{4EI\beta^3}F_4 - \frac{wF_{a6}}{8EI\beta^5(l-a)}
$$
