# TABLE 8.2 Reaction and deflection formulas for in-plane loading of elastic frames *(Continued)*

| 1b. Distributed load on the horizontal member | ![Figure 8.2-1b: Distributed load on the horizontal member](figures/fig8_2_1b.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a distributed load varying from $w_a$ to $w_b$ acting on the horizontal member. --> | $LP_H=-\dfrac{w_a l_3^3}{24E_3I_3}(l_1+l_2)-\dfrac{(w_b-w_a)l_3^3}{360E_3I_3}(7l_1+8l_2)$  $$LP_M=-\dfrac{7w_a l_3^3}{72E_3I_3}-\dfrac{11(w_b-w_a)l_3^3}{180E_3I_3}$$ |
| 1c. Concentrated moment on the horizontal member | ![Figure 8.2-1c: Concentrated moment on the horizontal member](figures/fig8_2_1c.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a concentrated moment $M_0$ applied at a distance $a$ along the horizontal member. --> | $LP_H=\dfrac{M_0}{6E_3I_3}\left[6l_1a-2l_1l_3-2l_2l_3-\dfrac{3a^2}{l_3}(l_1-l_2)\right]$  $$LP_M=\dfrac{M_0}{6E_3I_3}\left(4a-2l_3-\dfrac{3a^2}{l_3}\right)$$ |
| 1d. Concentrated angular displacement on the horizontal member | ![Figure 8.2-1d: Concentrated angular displacement on the horizontal member](figures/fig8_2_1d.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a concentrated angular displacement $\theta_0$ at a distance $a$ along the horizontal member. --> | $LP_H=\theta_0\left[l_1-\dfrac{a}{l_3}(l_1-l_2)\right]$  $$LP_M=\theta_0\left(\dfrac{2}{3}-\dfrac{a}{l_3}\right)$$ |
| 1e. Concentrated lateral displacement on the horizontal member | ![Figure 8.2-1e: Concentrated lateral displacement on the horizontal member](figures/fig8_2_1e.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a concentrated lateral displacement $\Delta_0$ at a distance $a$ along the horizontal member. --> | $LP_H=-\dfrac{\Delta_0(l_1-l_2)}{l_3}$  (Note: $\Delta_0$ could also be an increase in the length $l_1$ or a decrease in the length $l_2$)  $$LP_M=-\dfrac{\Delta_0}{l_3}$$ |
| 1f. Concentrated load on the left vertical member | ![Figure 8.2-1f: Concentrated load on the left vertical member](figures/fig8_2_1f.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a concentrated load $W$ applied on the left vertical member at a distance $a$. --> | $LP_H=W\left(A_{HH}-aA_{HM}+\dfrac{a^3}{6E_1I_1}\right)$  $$LP_M=W\left(A_{MH}-aA_{MM}+\dfrac{a^2}{2E_1I_1}\right)$$ |
| 1g. Distributed load on the left vertical member | ![Figure 8.2-1g: Distributed load on the left vertical member](figures/fig8_2_1g.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a distributed load varying from $w_a$ to $w_b$ on the left vertical member. --> | $LP_H=w_a\left(A_{HH}l_1-A_{HM}\dfrac{l_1^2}{2}+\dfrac{l_1^4}{24E_1I_1}\right)+(w_b-w_a)\left(A_{HH}\dfrac{l_1}{2}-A_{HM}\dfrac{l_1^2}{3}+\dfrac{l_1^4}{30E_1I_1}\right)$  $$LP_M=w_a\left[\dfrac{l_1^3}{6E_1I_1}+\dfrac{l_1l_3^3}{6E_3I_3}(l_1+l_2)\right]+(w_b-w_a)\left[\dfrac{l_1^3}{24E_1I_1}+\dfrac{l_1l_3^3}{36E_3I_3}(2l_1+3l_2)\right]$$ |
| 1h. Concentrated moment on the left vertical member | ![Figure 8.2-1h: Concentrated moment on the left vertical member](figures/fig8_2_1h.png) <!-- Description: A frame with a concentrated moment $M_0$ applied on the left vertical member at a distance $a$. --> | $LP_H=M_0\left(\dfrac{a^2}{2E_1I_1}-A_{HM}\right)$  $$LP_M=M_0\left(\dfrac{a}{E_1I_1}-A_{MM}\right)$$ |
