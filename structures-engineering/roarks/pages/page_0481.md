Case 5. Annular plate with a uniform line moment $M_o$ at a radius $r_o$

![Figure 11.2: Annular plate with a uniform line moment at radius ro](figures/fig11_2.png)
<!-- Description: Diagram of an annular plate showing inner radius b, outer radius a, applied line moment Mo at radius ro, edge moments Mrb and Mra, shears Qb and Qa, slopes thetab and thetaa, and deflection yb. -->

*Note:* If the loading $M_o$ is on the inside edge, $r > r_o$ everywhere, so $\langle r-r_o \rangle^0 = 1$ everywhere

General expressions for deformations, moments, and shears:

$$
y=y_b+\theta_brF_1+M_{rb}\frac{r^2}{D}F_2+Q_b\frac{r^3}{D}F_3+M_o\frac{r_o^2}{D}G_2
$$

$$
\theta=\theta_bF_4+M_{rb}\frac{r}{D}F_5+Q_b\frac{r^2}{D}F_6+M_o\frac{r_o}{D}G_5
$$

$$
M_r=\theta_b\frac{D}{r}F_7+M_{rb}F_8+Q_brF_9+M_oG_8
$$

$$
M_t=\frac{\theta D(1-\nu^2)}{r}+\nu M_r
$$

$$
Q=Q_b\frac{b}{r}
$$

For the numerical data given below, $\nu=0.3$

$$
y=K_y\frac{M_oa^2}{D}, \qquad \theta=K_\theta\frac{M_oa}{D}, \qquad M=K_M M_o, \qquad Q=K_Q\frac{M_o}{a}
$$
