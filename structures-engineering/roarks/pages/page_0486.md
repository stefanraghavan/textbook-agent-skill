## 6f. Outer edge fixed, inner edge guided

y_b = \theta_o a \left(\frac{C_2 L_4}{C_5} - L_1\right)

$$

## 6g. Outer edge fixed, inner edge simply supported

![Figure 11.2-6g: Outer edge fixed, inner edge simply supported](figures/fig11_2_6g.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with the outer edge fixed and inner edge simply supported, showing $r_o$ and rotation $\theta_0$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad M_{rb} = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0$

$$
\theta_b = \theta_o \frac{C_3 L_4 - C_6 L_1}{C_1 C_6 - C_3 C_4}
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{-\theta_o D}{a^2}\frac{C_1 L_4 - C_4 L_1}{C_1 C_6 - C_3 C_4}
$$

$$
M_{ra} = \theta_b \frac{D}{a} C_7 + Q_b a C_9 + \frac{\theta_o D}{a}L_7
$$

$$
Q_a = Q_b \frac{b}{a}
$$

## 6h. Outer edge fixed, inner edge fixed

![Figure 11.2-6h: Outer edge fixed, inner edge fixed](figures/fig11_2_6h.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with both outer and inner edges fixed, showing $r_o$ and rotation $\theta_0$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0$

$$
M_{rb} = \frac{\theta_o D}{a}\frac{C_3 L_4 - C_6 L_1}{C_2 C_6 - C_3 C_5}
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{-\theta_o D}{a^2}\frac{C_2 L_4 - C_5 L_1}{C_2 C_6 - C_3 C_5}
$$

$$
M_{ra} = M_{rb} C_8 + Q_b a C_9 + \theta_o \frac{D}{a}L_7
$$

$$
Q_a = Q_b \frac{b}{a}
$$
