Quasi-isotropic laminates are of the form (angles are in radians)

$$
\left[0/\frac{\pi}{n}/\frac{2\pi}{n}/\ldots/\frac{(n-1)\pi}{n}\right]_S
$$

or

$$
\left[\frac{\pi}{n}/\frac{2\pi}{n}/\ldots/\pi\right]_S
$$

where $n$ is any integer greater than 2. The two simplest and most common quasi-isotropic laminates are $[0/\pm 60]_S$ and $[0/\pm 45/90]_S$, illustrated in Figure C.10(a) and $(b)$, respectively.
