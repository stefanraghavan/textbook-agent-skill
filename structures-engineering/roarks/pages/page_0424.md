# TABLE 10.3 Formulas for the elastic deformations of uniform thin-walled open members under torsional loading *(Continued)*

## 1c. Left end free to twist but not warp, right end free to warp but not twist

![Figure 10.3-1c: Left end free to twist but not warp, right end free to warp but not twist](figures/fig10_3_1c.png)
<!-- Description: A thin-walled open member with torque $T_o$ applied near the left end at distance $a$; left end restrained against warping but free to twist, right end restrained against twisting but free to warp. -->

$$
T_A=0,\qquad \theta'_A=0
$$

$$
\theta_A=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^3}\left(\frac{C_3 C_{a2}}{C_1}-C_{a4}\right)
$$

$$
\theta''_A=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E \beta}\frac{C_{a2}}{C_1}
$$

$$
\theta_B=0,\qquad \theta''_B=0,\qquad T_B=-T_o
$$

$$
\theta'_B=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E \beta^2}\left(\frac{C_2 C_{a2}}{C_1}-C_{a3}\right)
$$

$$
\theta'''_B=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E}\left(\frac{C_2 C_{a2}}{C_1}-C_{a1}\right)
$$

If $a=0$ (torque applied at the left end),

$$
\theta=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^3}\left[\sinh \beta x-\tanh \beta l \cosh \beta x+\beta(l-x)\right]
$$

$$
\theta'=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^2}\left(\cosh \beta x-\tanh \beta l \sinh \beta x-1\right)
$$

$$
\theta''=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta}\left(\sinh \beta x-\tanh \beta l \cosh \beta x\right),\qquad
\theta'''=\frac{T_o}{C_w E}\left(\cosh \beta x-\tanh \beta l \sinh \beta x\right)
$$

$$
\theta_{\max}=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^3}\left(\beta l-\tanh \beta l\right)\qquad \text{at } x=0
$$

$$
\theta'_{\max}=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E \beta^2}\left(\frac{-1}{\cosh \beta l}+1\right)\qquad \text{at } x=l
$$

$$
\theta''_{\max}=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E \beta}\tanh \beta l\qquad \text{at } x=0
$$

$$
\theta'''_{\max}=\frac{T_o}{C_w E}\qquad \text{at } x=0
$$

## 1d. Left end free to twist but not warp, right end fixed (no twist or warp)

![Figure 10.3-1d: Left end free to twist but not warp, right end fixed](figures/fig10_3_1d.png)
<!-- Description: A thin-walled open member with torque $T_o$ applied near the left end at distance $a$; left end restrained against warping but free to twist, right end fully fixed against twist and warp. -->

$$
T_A=0,\qquad \theta'_A=0
$$

$$
\theta_A=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^3}\left(\frac{C_3 C_{a3}}{C_2}-C_{a4}\right)
$$

$$
\theta''_A=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E \beta}\frac{C_{a3}}{C_2}
$$

$$
\theta_B=0,\qquad \theta'_B=0,\qquad T_B=-T_o
$$

$$
\theta''_B=\frac{-T_o}{C_w E \beta}\left(\frac{C_1 C_{a3}}{C_2}-C_{a2}\right)
$$

$$
\theta'''_B=\frac{T_o}{C_w E}
$$

If $a=0$ (torque applied at the left end),

$$
\theta=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^3}\left[\sinh \beta x+\beta(l-x)-\tanh \frac{\beta l}{2}(1+\cosh \beta x)\right]
$$

$$
\theta'=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^2}\left(\cosh \beta x-1-\tanh \frac{\beta l}{2}\sinh \beta x\right)
$$

$$
\theta''=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta}\left(\sinh \beta x-\tanh \frac{\beta l}{2}\cosh \beta x\right),\qquad
\theta'''=\frac{T_o}{C_w E}\left(\cosh \beta x-\tanh \frac{\beta l}{2}\sinh \beta x\right)
$$

$$
\theta_{\max}=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^3}\left(\beta l-2\tanh \frac{\beta l}{2}\right)\qquad \text{at } x=0
$$

$$
\theta'_{\max}=\frac{T_o}{C_w E \beta^2}\left[\frac{1}{\cosh(\beta l/2)}-1\right]\qquad \text{at } x=\frac{l}{2}
$$

$$
\theta''_{\max}=\frac{\mp T_o}{C_w E \beta}\tanh \frac{\beta l}{2}\qquad \text{at } x=0 \text{ and } x=l,\ \text{respectively}
$$

$$
\theta'''_{\max}=\frac{T_o}{C_w E}\qquad \text{at } x=0 \text{ and } x=l
$$
