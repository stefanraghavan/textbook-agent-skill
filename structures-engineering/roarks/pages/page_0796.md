## 20. Infinite row of circular holes in an infinite plate

![Figure 20: Infinite row of circular holes in an infinite plate](figures/fig20.png)
<!-- Description: A plate with an infinite row of equally spaced circular holes, showing spacing $L$, hole radius $r$, and applied stresses $\sigma_1$ and $\sigma_2$. -->

### 20a. Elastic stress, axial tension parallel to the row of holes

$$
\sigma_{\max}=K_t\sigma_1,\qquad \sigma_2=0
$$

where

$$
K_t=3.0-1.061\left(\frac{2r}{L}\right)-2.136\left(\frac{2r}{L}\right)^2+1.877\left(\frac{2r}{L}\right)^3
$$

(Refs. 1 and 57)

### 20b. Elastic stress, axial tension normal to the row of holes

$$
\sigma_{\max}=K_t\sigma_2,\qquad \sigma_1=0
$$

where

$$
K_t=3.0-3.057\left(\frac{2r}{L}\right)+0.214\left(\frac{2r}{L}\right)^2+0.843\left(\frac{2r}{L}\right)^3
$$

(Refs. 1 and 57)

## 21. Gear tooth

![Figure 21: Gear tooth](figures/fig21.png)
<!-- Description: Gear tooth profile with labeled points A, B, C, pressure angle $\phi$, forces $P$ and $P\tan\phi$, dimensions $t$, $h$, $r$, and tooth width $b$. -->

Elastic stress, bending plus some compression

For $14.5^\circ$ pressure angle:

$$
K_t=0.22+\left(\frac{t}{r}\right)^{0.2}\left(\frac{t}{h}\right)^{0.4}
$$

For $20^\circ$ pressure angle:

$$
K_t=0.18+\left(\frac{t}{r}\right)^{0.15}\left(\frac{t}{h}\right)^{0.45}
$$

$$
K_t=[(\sigma_t)_{\max}\text{ by photoelastic analysis}]/\left[\text{calculated }(\sigma_t)_{\max}=\frac{6Ph}{bt^2}-\frac{P\tan\phi}{bt}\right]
$$

(Alternatively, the maximum stress can be found by the formula for a short cantilever, pages 168 and 169.)

$A$ and $C$ are points of tangency of the inscribed parabola $ABC$ with tooth profile,  
$b$ = tooth width normal to plane of figure, $r$ = minimum radius of tooth fillet.

(Ref. 58)
