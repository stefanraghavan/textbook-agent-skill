### 8c. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge simply supported

![Figure 8c: Outer edge simply supported, inner edge simply supported](figures/fig8c.png)
<!-- Description: Circular plate edge-condition sketch with both outer and inner edges simply supported, inner radius $r_o$, and temperature variation labeled $T-\frac{\Delta T}{2}$ and $T+\frac{\Delta T}{2}$. -->

$$
y_b = 0,\quad M_{rb} = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0
$$

$$
\theta_b = -\frac{\gamma(1+\nu)\Delta T a}{t}\frac{C_9L_2 + C_3(1-L_8)}{C_1C_9 - C_3C_7}
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{\gamma(1+\nu)\Delta T D}{at}\frac{C_7L_2 + C_1(1-L_8)}{C_1C_9 - C_3C_7}
$$

$$
\theta_a = \theta_b C_4 + Q_b\frac{a^2}{D}C_6 + \frac{\gamma(1+\nu)\Delta T a}{t}L_5
$$

$$
Q_a = Q_b\frac{b}{a}
$$

### 8d. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge fixed

y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0
