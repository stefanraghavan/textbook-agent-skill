## 23. Hollow semicircle

![Figure 23: Hollow semicircle](figures/fig23.png)
<!-- Description: A hollow semicircle with outer radius R and inner radius Ri, with axes x and y shown and distances yc1, yc2, and xc indicated. -->

Note:
$$
b=\frac{R+R_i}{2}
$$

$$
t=R-R_i
$$

$$
A=\frac{\pi}{2}(R^2-R_i^2)
$$

$$
y_{c2}=\frac{4}{3\pi}\frac{R^3-R_i^3}{R^2-R_i^2}
$$

or

$$
y_{c2}=\frac{2b}{\pi}\left[1+\frac{(t/b)^2}{12}\right]
$$

$$
y_{c1}=R-y_{c2}
$$

$$
x_c=R
$$

$$
I_x=\frac{\pi}{8}(R^4-R_i^4)-\frac{8}{9\pi}\frac{(R^3-R_i^3)^2}{R^2-R_i^2}
$$

or

$$
I_x=0.2976tb^3+0.1805bt^3-\frac{0.00884t^5}{b}
$$

$$
I_y=\frac{\pi}{8}(R^4-R_i^4)
$$

or

$$
I_y=1.5708b^3t+0.3927bt^3
$$

Let $y_p$ be the vertical distance from the bottom to the plastic neutral axis.

$$
y_p=(0.7071-0.2716C-0.4299C^2+0.3983C^3)R
$$

$$
Z_x=(0.8284-0.9140C+0.7245C^2-0.2850C^3)R^2t
$$

where $C=t/R$

$$
Z_y=0.6667(R^3-R_i^3)
$$
