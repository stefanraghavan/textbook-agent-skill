# TABLE 8.5 Shear, moment, slope, and deflection formulas for finite-length beams on elastic foundations *(Continued)*

**7. Uniform temperature differential from top to bottom**

![Figure 8.5: Uniform temperature differential from top to bottom beam diagram](figures/fig8_5.png)
<!-- Description: Beam on elastic foundation with left-end quantities $M_A$, $R_A$, $y_A$, $\theta_A$, vertical axis $Y$, horizontal axis $X$, beam length $l$, and temperature labels $T_1$ on top and $T_2$ on bottom. -->

Transverse shear
$$
V = R_A F_1 - y_A 2EI\beta^3 F_2 - \theta_A 2EI\beta^2 F_3 - M_A \beta F_4 + \frac{T_1-T_2}{t}\gamma EI\beta F_4
$$

Bending moment
$$
M = M_A F_1 + \frac{R_A}{2\beta}F_2 - y_A 2EI\beta^2 F_3 - \theta_A EI\beta F_4 - \frac{T_1-T_2}{t}\gamma EI(F_1-1)
$$

Slope
$$
\theta = \theta_A F_1 + \frac{M_A}{2EI\beta}F_2 + \frac{R_A}{2EI\beta^2}F_3 - y_A \beta F_4 - \frac{T_1-T_2}{2t\beta}\gamma F_2
$$

Deflection
$$
y = y_A F_1 + \frac{\theta_A}{2\beta}F_2 + \frac{M_A}{2EI\beta^2}F_3 + \frac{R_A}{4EI\beta^3}F_4 - \frac{T_1-T_2}{2t\beta^2}\gamma F_3
$$
