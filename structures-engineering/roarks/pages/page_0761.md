SEC. 16.4] Dynamic and Temperature Stresses 757

2. A moving body of mass $M$ strikes transversely the center of a simple beam of mass $M_1$. Then

$$
K=\frac{1+\dfrac{17M_1}{25M}}{\left(1+\dfrac{5M_1}{8M}\right)^2}
$$

If there is a body of mass $M_2$ attached to the beam at its center, then

$$
K=\frac{1+\dfrac{17M_1}{35M}+\dfrac{M_2}{M}}{\left(1+\dfrac{5M_1}{8M}+\dfrac{M_2}{M}\right)^2}
$$

3. A moving body of mass $M$ strikes transversely the end of a cantilever beam of mass $M_1$. Then

$$
K=\frac{1+\dfrac{33M_1}{140M}}{\left(1+\dfrac{3M_1}{8M}\right)^2}
$$

If there is a body of mass $M_2$ attached to the beam at the struck end, then

$$
K=\frac{1+\dfrac{33M_1}{140M}+\dfrac{M_2}{M}}{\left(1+\dfrac{3M_1}{8M}+\dfrac{M_2}{M}\right)^2}
$$

4. A moving body of mass $M$ strikes transversely the center of a beam with fixed ends and of mass $M_1$. Then

$$
K=\frac{1+\dfrac{13M_1}{35M}}{\left(1+\dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{M_1}{M}\right)^2}
$$

If there is a body of mass $M_2$ attached to the beam at the center, then

$$
K=\frac{1+\dfrac{13M_1}{35M}+\dfrac{M_2}{M}}{\left(1+\dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{M_1}{M}+\dfrac{M_2}{M}\right)^2}
$$
