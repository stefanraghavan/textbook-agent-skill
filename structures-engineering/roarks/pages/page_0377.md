**4h. Right end supported and slope-guided, left end simply supported**

![Figure 9.4h: Right end supported and slope-guided, left end simply supported](figures/fig9_4h.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with distributed load w acting downward over the right portion, vertical reaction V_A at the left end, vertical reaction V_B and moment M_B at the right end. Boundary conditions shown: M_A = 0, T_A = 0, y_A = 0, T_B = 0, y_B = 0, \Theta_B = 0. -->

$M_A=0 \qquad T_A=0 \qquad y_A=0$

$T_B=0 \qquad y_B=0 \qquad \Theta_B=0$

$$
V_A=wR\frac{\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=\frac{wR^3}{EI}\left\{\frac{C_{a13}\sin\phi+C_6[\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)]}{1-\cos\phi}
-\frac{C_3\sin\phi[\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)]}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}
-C_{a16}\right\}
$$

$$
\psi_A=\frac{wR^3}{EI}\left\{\frac{C_{a16}\sin\phi-C_{a13}\cos\phi}{1-\cos\phi}
-(C_6\sin\phi-C_3\cos\phi)\frac{\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}\right\}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A-wR(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi-wR^2[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
\psi_B=\psi_A\cos\phi-\Theta_A\sin\phi+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9-\frac{wR^3}{EI}C_{a19}
$$

**4i. Right end supported and roll-guided, left end supported and roll-guided**

![Figure 9.4i: Right end supported and roll-guided, left end supported and roll-guided](figures/fig9_4i.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with distributed load w acting downward over the right portion, vertical reaction V_A and tangential reaction T_A at the left end, vertical reaction V_B and tangential reaction T_B at the right end. Boundary conditions shown: M_A = 0, y_A = 0, \psi_A = 0, M_B = 0, y_B = 0, \psi_B = 0. -->

$M_A=0 \qquad y_A=0 \qquad \psi_A=0$

$M_B=0 \qquad y_B=0 \qquad \psi_B=0$

$$
V_A=wR\frac{(C_{a13}+C_{a19})\sin\phi+(C_2+C_8)[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
T_A=wR^2\frac{(C_{a13}+C_{a19})\sin\phi-(C_3+C_9)[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A=\frac{wR^3}{EI}\frac{C_{a13}(C_8+C_9)-C_{a19}(C_2+C_3)+(C_2C_9-C_3C_8)[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]/\sin\phi}{(C_2+C_3+C_8+C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A-wR(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B=V_AR(1-\cos\phi)+T_A\cos\phi-wR^2[\phi-\theta-\sin(\phi-\theta)]
$$

$$
\Theta_B=\Theta_A\cos\phi+\frac{T_AR}{EI}C_5+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_6-\frac{wR^3}{EI}C_{a16}
$$
