**4.** Uniform straight bar under end load $P'$; both ends hinged and bar elastically supported by lateral pressure $p$ proportional to deflection $(p = ky$, where $k =$ lateral force per unit length per unit of deflection)

$$
P' = \frac{\pi^2 EI}{l^2}\left(m^2 + \frac{kl^4}{m^2\pi^4 EI}\right)
$$

where $m$ represents the number of half-waves into which the bar buckles and is equal to the lowest integer greater than

$$
\frac{1}{2}\left(\sqrt{1 + \frac{4l^2}{\pi^2}\sqrt{\frac{k}{EI}}} - 1\right)
$$
