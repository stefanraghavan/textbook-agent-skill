## 5b. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge guided

![Figure 5b](figures/fig5b.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge simply supported and inner edge guided, showing radius $r_o$ and applied moment $M_o$. -->

$\theta_b = 0,\quad Q_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0$

$$
y_b = \frac{M_o a^2}{D}\left(\frac{C_2 L_8}{C_8} - L_2\right)
$$

$$
M_{rb} = \frac{-M_o L_8}{C_8}
$$

$$
\theta_a = \frac{-M_o a}{D}\left(\frac{C_5 L_8}{C_8} - L_5\right)
$$

$$
Q_a = 0
$$

$y_{\max} = y_b,\quad M_{\max} = M_{rb}$

## 5c. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge simply supported

![Figure 5c](figures/fig5c.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge simply supported and inner edge simply supported, showing radius $r_o$ and applied moment $M_o$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad M_{rb} = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0$

$$
\theta_b = \frac{M_o a}{D}\frac{C_3 L_8 - C_9 L_2}{C_1 C_9 - C_3 C_7}
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{-M_o}{a}\frac{C_1 L_8 - C_7 L_2}{C_1 C_9 - C_3 C_7}
$$

$$
\theta_a = \theta_b C_4 + Q_b \frac{a^2}{D} C_6 + \frac{M_o a}{D}L_5
$$

$$
Q_a = Q_b \frac{b}{a}
$$

## 5d. Outer edge simply supported, inner edge fixed

![Figure 5d](figures/fig5d.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge simply supported and inner edge fixed, showing radius $r_o$ and applied moment $M_o$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad y_a = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0$

$$
M_{rb} = M_o \frac{C_3 L_8 - C_9 L_2}{C_2 C_9 - C_3 C_8}
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{-M_o}{a}\frac{C_2 L_8 - C_8 L_2}{C_2 C_9 - C_3 C_8}
$$

$$
\theta_a = M_{rb}\frac{a}{D}C_5 + Q_b\frac{a^2}{D}C_6 + \frac{M_o a}{D}L_5
$$

$$
Q_a = Q_b \frac{b}{a}
$$
