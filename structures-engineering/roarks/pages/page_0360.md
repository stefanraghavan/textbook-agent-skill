## 1g. Right end supported and slope-guided, left end supported and roll-guided

![Figure 1g](figures/fig1g.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with downward load W, left-end reactions $T_A$ and $V_A$, right-end reaction $V_B$ and moment $M_B$. Boundary conditions shown below the figure. -->

$M_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \psi_A = 0$

$T_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0$

$$
V_A = W \frac{(C_5 \sin \phi - C_2 \cos \phi)[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)] + C_{a3}\cos^2\phi - C_{a6}\sin\phi\cos\phi}{(C_5 \sin \phi - C_2 \cos \phi)(1-\cos\phi) + C_3 \cos^2\phi - C_6 \sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
T_A = WR \frac{(C_3 \cos \phi - C_6 \sin \phi)[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)] - (C_{a3}\cos\phi - C_{a6}\sin\phi)(1-\cos\phi)}{(C_5 \sin \phi - C_2 \cos \phi)(1-\cos\phi) + C_3 \cos^2\phi - C_6 \sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = \frac{WR^2}{EI}
\frac{(C_2C_6 - C_3C_5)[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)] + (C_{a3}C_5 - C_{a6}C_2)(1-\cos\phi) + (C_{a6}C_3 - C_{a3}C_6)\cos\phi}{(C_5 \sin \phi - C_2 \cos \phi)(1-\cos\phi) + C_3 \cos^2\phi - C_6 \sin\phi\cos\phi}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A - W
$$

$$
M_B = V_A R \sin\phi - T_A \sin\phi - WR\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\psi_B = -\Theta_A \sin\phi + \frac{T_A R}{EI} C_8 + \frac{V_A R^2}{EI} C_9 - \frac{WR^2}{EI} C_{a9}
$$

## 1h. Right end supported and slope-guided, left end simply supported

![Figure 1h](figures/fig1h.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with downward load W, left-end vertical reaction $V_A$, right-end vertical reaction $V_B$ and moment $M_B$. Boundary conditions shown below the figure. -->

$M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0$

$T_B = 0 \qquad y_B = 0 \qquad \Theta_B = 0$

$$
V_A = W \frac{1-\cos(\phi-\theta)}{1-\cos\phi}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = \frac{WR^2}{EI}\left\{\frac{C_{a3}\sin\phi + C_6[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]}{1-\cos\phi} - \frac{C_3\sin\phi[1-\cos(\phi-\theta)]}{(1-\cos\phi)^2} - C_{a6}\right\}
$$

$$
\psi_A = \frac{WR^2}{EI}\left\{\frac{C_{a6}\sin\phi - C_{a3}\cos\phi}{1-\cos\phi} - (C_6\sin\phi - C_3\cos\phi)\frac{1-\cos(\phi-\theta)}{(1-\cos\phi)^2}\right\}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A - W
$$

$$
M_B = V_A R \sin\phi - WR\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\psi_B = \psi_A \cos\phi - \Theta_A \sin\phi + \frac{V_A R^2}{EI} C_9 - \frac{WR^2}{EI} C_{a9}
$$
