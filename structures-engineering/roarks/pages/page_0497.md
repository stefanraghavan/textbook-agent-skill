| 17. Uniform load over a very small central circular area of radius $r_o$, edge fixed | For $r > r_o'$  $$y=-\frac{W}{16\pi D}\left[a^2-r^2\left(1+2\ln\frac{a}{r}\right)\right]$$  $$\theta=\frac{Wr}{4\pi D}\ln\frac{a}{r}$$  $$M_r=\frac{W}{4\pi}\left[(1+\nu)\ln\frac{a}{r}-1+\frac{(1-\nu)r_o'^2}{4r^2}\right]$$  where $r_o'=\sqrt{1.6r_o^2+t^2}-0.675t \quad$ if $r_o<0.5t$  or $\quad r_o'=r_o \quad$ if $r_o\geq 0.5t$  $$M_t=\frac{W}{4\pi}\left[(1+\nu)\ln\frac{a}{r}-\nu+\frac{\nu(1-\nu)r_o'^2}{4r^2}\right]$$ | $$y_{\max}=-\frac{Wa^2}{16\pi D} \qquad \text{at } r=0$$  $$\theta_{\max}=0.0293\,\frac{Wa}{D} \qquad \text{at } r=0.368a$$  $$(+M_r)_{\max}=\frac{W}{4\pi r_o'}(1+\nu)\ln\frac{a}{r_o'} \qquad \text{at } r=0$$  $$(-M_r)_{\max}=-\frac{W}{4\pi} \qquad \text{at } r=a$$  $$(+M_t)_{\max}=(+M_r)_{\max} \qquad \text{at } r=0$$  $$(-M_t)_{\max}=-\frac{\nu W}{4\pi} \qquad \text{at } r=a$$ |

| 18. Uniform load over a small eccentric circular area of radius $r_o$; edge simply supported | $$(M_r)_{\max}=(M_t)_{\max}=\frac{W}{4\pi}\left[1+(1+\nu)\ln\left(\frac{a-p}{r_o'}\right)-\frac{(1-\nu)r_o'^2}{4(a-p)^2}\right] \quad \text{at the load}$$  At any point $s$,  $$M_r=(M_r)_{\max}\,\frac{(1+\nu)\ln(a_1/r_1)}{1+(1+\nu)\ln(a_1/r_o')} , \qquad M_t=(M_t)_{\max}\,\frac{(1+\nu)\ln(a_1/r_1)+1-\nu}{1+(1+\nu)\ln(a_1/r_o')}$$  $$y=-\left[K_o(r^3-b_or^2+c_oa^3)+K_1(r^4-b_1ar^3+c_1a^3r)\cos\phi+K_2(r^4-b_2ar^3+c_2a^2r^2)\cos2\phi\right]$$  where $$K_o=\frac{W}{\pi Da^4}\frac{2(1+\nu)}{9(5+\nu)}(p^3-b_oa p^2+c_oa^3)$$  $$K_1=\frac{W}{\pi Da^6}\frac{2(3+\nu)}{3(9+\nu)}(p^4-b_1ap^3+c_1a^3p)$$  $$K_2=\frac{W}{\pi Da^6}\frac{(4+\nu)^2}{(9+\nu)(5+\nu)}(p^4-b_2ap^3+c_2a^2p^2)$$  where $$b_o=\frac{3(2+\nu)}{2(1+\nu)}, \qquad b_1=\frac{3(4+\nu)}{2(3+\nu)}, \qquad b_2=\frac{2(5+\nu)}{4+\nu}, \qquad c_o=\frac{4+\nu}{2(1+\nu)}, \qquad c_1=\frac{6+\nu}{2(3+\nu)}, \qquad c_2=\frac{6+\nu}{4+\nu}$$  (See Ref. 1) |  |
