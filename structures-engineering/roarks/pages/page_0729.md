5. Uniform straight bar under end load $P$; both ends hinged and bar elastically supported by lateral pressure $p$ proportional to deflection but where the constant of proportionality depends upon the direction of the deflection ($p = k_1 y$ for deflection toward the softer foundation; $p = k_2 y$ for deflection toward the harder foundation); these are also called unattached foundations

![Figure 15.1: Uniform straight bar under end load with hinged ends and unequal lateral elastic supports](figures/fig15_1.png)
<!-- Description: A vertical bar of length l with hinged ends, compressed by load P at the top and bottom. Lateral pressure acts on the left as p = k1y and on the right as p = k2y, with the bar deflected sideways. -->

$$
P' = \frac{\pi^2 EI}{l^2}\left(m^2 + \frac{k_2 l^4}{m^2 \pi^4 EI}\phi^\alpha\right)
$$

where

$$
\phi = \frac{k_1}{k_2}
$$

and $\alpha$ depends upon $m$ as given below

| $m$ | $\alpha$ |
|---|---|
| 1 | 1 |
| 2 | $1 + \phi(0.23 - 0.017l^2\sqrt{k_2/EI})$ |
| 3 | $0.75 - 0.56\phi$ |

This is an empirical expression which closely fits numerical solutions found in Ref. 45 and is valid only over the range $0 \le l^2\sqrt{k_2/EI} \le 120$. Solutions for $P'$ are carried out for values of $m = 1, 2,$ and 3, and the lowest one governs
