## 1d. Uniformly increasing along length

![Figure 1d](figures/fig1d.png)
<!-- Description: A rectangular plate with a triangular distributed load increasing uniformly along the length a, from zero at one end to q at the other. -->

$\sigma_{\max} = \dfrac{\beta q b^2}{t^2}$ and $y_{\max} = \dfrac{-\alpha q b^4}{Et^3}$

| $a/b$ | 1 | 1.5 | 2.0 | 2.5 | 3.0 | 3.5 | 4.0 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $\beta$ | 0.16 | 0.26 | 0.34 | 0.38 | 0.43 | 0.47 | 0.49 |
| $\alpha$ | 0.022 | 0.043 | 0.060 | 0.070 | 0.078 | 0.086 | 0.091 |

(Values from charts of Ref. 8; $\nu = 0.3$.)
