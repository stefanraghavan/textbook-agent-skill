| 5p. Concentrated angular displacement

![Figure 5p. Concentrated angular displacement](figures/fig5p.png)
<!-- Description: A circular arch with a concentrated angular displacement $\theta_0$ shown at a section; angle $\phi$ is marked between dashed and vertical reference lines. --> | $LF_H=\dfrac{\theta_0 EI}{R^2}(m-c)$

| 5q. Concentrated radial displacement

![Figure 5q. Concentrated radial displacement](figures/fig5q.png)

$LF_V=\dfrac{\Delta_0 EI}{R^3}(m)$

$LF_M=0$ |
| 5r. Uniform temperature rise over that span to the right of point $Q$

![Figure 5r. Uniform temperature rise over that span to the right of point Q](figures/fig5r.png)
