## 4j. Outer edge guided, inner edge fixed

![Figure 4j](figures/fig4j.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge guided and inner edge fixed, showing radius $r_o$ and parabolically increasing load $q$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0,\quad Q_a = 0$

$$
M_{rb} = -\frac{qa^2}{C_5}\left[\frac{C_6}{12ab}(3a^2 - 2ar_o - r_o^2) - L_{16}\right]
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{q}{12b}(3a^2 - 2ar_o - r_o^2)
$$

$$
y_a = M_{rb}\frac{a^2}{D}C_2 + Q_b\frac{a^3}{D}C_3 - \frac{qa^4}{D}L_{13}
$$

$$
M_{ra} = M_{rb}C_8 + Q_baC_9 - qa^2L_{19}
$$

If $r_o = b$ (parabolically increasing load from $b$ to $a$),

| $b/a$ | 0.1 | 0.3 | 0.5 | 0.7 | 0.9 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $K_{y_a}$ | $-0.0199$ | $-0.0070$ | $-0.0017$ | $-0.0002$ |  |
| $K_{M_{rb}}$ | $-0.4081$ | $-0.1490$ | $-0.0573$ | $-0.0169$ | $-0.00161$ |
| $K_{M_{ra}}$ | $0.0745$ | $0.0485$ | $0.0253$ | $0.0091$ | $0.00100$ |

## 4k. Outer edge free, inner edge simply supported

![Figure 4k](figures/fig4k.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge free and inner edge simply supported, showing radius $r_o$ and parabolically increasing load $q$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad M_{rb} = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0,\quad Q_a = 0$

$$
\theta_a = -\frac{qa^3}{DC_7}\left[\frac{C_9}{12ab}(3a^2 - 2ar_o - r_o^2) - L_{19}\right]
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{q}{12b}(3a^2 - 2ar_o - r_o^2)
$$

$$
y_a = \theta_b aC_1 + Q_b\frac{a^3}{D}C_3 - \frac{qa^4}{D}L_{13}
$$

$$
\theta_a = \theta_b C_4 + Q_b\frac{a^2}{D}C_6 - \frac{qa^3}{D}L_{16}
$$
