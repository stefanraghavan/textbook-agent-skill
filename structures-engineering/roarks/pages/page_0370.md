## 3e. Right end fixed, left end fixed

![Figure 3e](figures/fig3e.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with both ends fixed and applied torque $T_0$; boundary conditions shown for ends A and B. -->

$y_A=0 \qquad \theta_A=0 \qquad \psi_A=0$

$y_B=0 \qquad \theta_B=0 \qquad \psi_B=0$

$$
V_A=-\frac{T_0}{R}\frac{C_{a2}(C_4C_8-C_5C_7)+C_{a5}(C_2C_7-C_1C_8)+C_{a8}(C_1C_5-C_2C_4)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
M_A=-T_0\frac{C_{a2}(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_{a5}(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_{a8}(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
T_A=-T_0\frac{C_{a2}(C_6C_7-C_4C_9)+C_{a5}(C_1C_9-C_3C_7)+C_{a8}(C_3C_4-C_1C_6)}{C_1(C_5C_9-C_6C_8)+C_4(C_3C_8-C_2C_9)+C_7(C_2C_6-C_3C_5)}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi+M_A\cos\phi-T_A\sin\phi-T_0\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B=V_AR(1-\cos\phi)+M_A\sin\phi+T_A\cos\phi+T_0\cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

## 3f. Right end supported and slope-guided, left end supported and slope-guided

![Figure 3f](figures/fig3f.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with end forces and moments $V_A$, $M_A$, $V_B$, $M_B$, and applied torque $T_0$; both ends supported and slope-guided. -->

$T_A=0 \qquad y_A=0 \qquad \theta_A=0$

$T_B=0 \qquad y_B=0 \qquad \theta_B=0$

$$
V_A=\frac{T_0}{R}\frac{[C_1\sin\phi-C_4(1-\cos\phi)]\cos(\phi-\theta)-C_{a2}\sin^2\phi+C_{a5}(1-\cos\phi)\sin\phi}{C_4(1-\cos\phi)^2+C_3\sin^2\phi-(C_1+C_6)(1-\cos\phi)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
M_A=-T_0\frac{[C_3\sin\phi-C_6(1-\cos\phi)]\cos(\phi-\theta)-C_{a2}(1-\cos\phi)\sin\phi+C_{a5}(1-\cos\phi)^2}{C_4(1-\cos\phi)^2+C_3\sin^2\phi-(C_1+C_6)(1-\cos\phi)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
\psi_A=\frac{T_0R}{EI}\frac{(C_3C_4-C_1C_6)\cos(\phi-\theta)+(C_{a2}C_6-C_{a5}C_3)\sin\phi-(C_{a2}C_4-C_{a5}C_1)(1-\cos\phi)}{C_4(1-\cos\phi)^2+C_3\sin^2\phi-(C_1+C_6)(1-\cos\phi)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
V_B=V_A
$$

$$
M_B=V_AR\sin\phi+M_A\cos\phi-T_0\sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
\psi_B=\psi_A\cos\phi+\frac{M_AR}{EI}C_7+\frac{V_AR^2}{EI}C_9+\frac{T_0R}{EI}C_{a8}
$$
