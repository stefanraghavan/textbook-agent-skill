$$
LT_M=-wR^2[1-\cos(x-\theta)]\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

$$
LT_N=-wR[1-\cos(x-\theta)]\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

$$
LT_V=-wR\sin(x-\theta)\langle x-\theta\rangle^0
$$

$$
M_A=\frac{-wR^2}{\pi}\left[s+\pi c-\theta c-k_2(\pi-\theta-s)\right]
$$

$$
M_C=\frac{-wR^2}{\pi}\left[\pi-s+\theta c-k_2(\pi-\theta-s)\right]
$$

$$
N_A=\frac{-wR}{\pi}(s+\pi c-\theta c)
$$

$$
V_A=0
$$

$$
\Delta_H=
\begin{cases}
\dfrac{-wR^4}{EI}\left[\dfrac{k_1(s+\theta c)}{2}+2k_2(1-s)-\dfrac{2k_2^2(\pi-\theta-s)}{\pi}\right] & \text{for }\theta\le \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\[1.2em]
\dfrac{-wR^4}{EI}\left[\dfrac{k_1(s+\pi c-\theta c)}{2}-\dfrac{2k_2^2(\pi-\theta-s)}{\pi}\right] & \text{for }\theta\ge \dfrac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\Delta_V=\frac{wR^4}{EI}\left[\frac{k_1s(\pi-\theta)}{2}-k_2(1+c)+\frac{2k_2^2(\pi-\theta-s)}{\pi}\right]
$$

$$
\Delta L=
\begin{cases}
\dfrac{wR^4}{2EI\pi}\left[k_1\left(\dfrac{\pi^2 s}{2}-s+\theta c\right)+k_2\pi(\theta-s-2)+2k_2^2(\pi-\theta-s)\right] & \text{for }\theta\le \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\[1.2em]
\dfrac{wR^4}{2EI\pi}\left[k_1(\pi^2 s-\pi\theta s-\pi c-s+\theta c)+k_2\pi(\pi-\theta-s-2-2c)+2k_2^2(\pi-\theta-s)\right] & \text{for }\theta\ge \dfrac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}
$$

Max $+\,M$ occurs at an angular position $x_1$ where $x_1>\theta$, $x_1>90^\circ$, and

$$
x_1=\tan^{-1}\frac{-\pi s}{s-\theta c}
$$

Max $-\,M=M_C$

If $\alpha=\beta=0$, $M=K_MwR^2$, $N=K_NwR$, $\Delta D=K_{\Delta D}wR^4/EI$, etc.
