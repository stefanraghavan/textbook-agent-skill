## TABLE 10.1 Formulas for torsional deformation and stress

GENERAL FORMULAS: $\theta = TL/KG$ and $\tau = T/Q$, where $\theta =$ angle of twist (radians); $T =$ twisting moment (force-length); $L =$ length, $\tau =$ unit shear stress (force per unit area); $G =$ modulus of rigidity (force per unit area); $K$ (length to the fourth) and $Q$ (length cubed) are functions of the cross section

| Form and dimensions of cross sections, other quantities involved, and case no. | Formula for $K$ in $\theta = \dfrac{TL}{KG}$ | Formula for shear stress |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Solid circular section  ![Figure 10.1: Solid circular section](figures/fig10_1.png) <!-- Description: A solid circle with diameter labeled 2r. --> | $K = \dfrac{1}{2}\pi r^4$ | $\tau_{\max} = \dfrac{2T}{\pi r^3}$ at boundary |
| 2. Solid elliptical section  ![Figure 10.2: Solid elliptical section](figures/fig10_2.png) <!-- Description: A solid ellipse with horizontal major axis labeled 2a and vertical minor axis labeled 2b. --> | $K = \dfrac{\pi a^3 b^3}{a^2 + b^2}$ | $\tau_{\max} = \dfrac{2T}{\pi a b^2}$ at ends of minor axis |
| 3. Solid square section  ![Figure 10.3: Solid square section](figures/fig10_3.png) <!-- Description: A square with side dimension labeled 2a. --> | $K = 2.25a^4$ | $\tau_{\max} = \dfrac{0.601T}{a^3}$ at midpoint of each side |
| 4. Solid rectangular section  ![Figure 10.4: Solid rectangular section](figures/fig10_4.png) <!-- Description: A rectangle with horizontal dimension labeled 2a and vertical dimension labeled 2b. --> | $K = ab^3\left[\dfrac{16}{3} - 3.36\dfrac{b}{a}\left(1 - \dfrac{b^4}{12a^4}\right)\right]$ for $a \geq b$ | $\tau_{\max} = \dfrac{3T}{8ab^2}\left[1 + 0.6095\dfrac{b}{a} + 0.8865\left(\dfrac{b}{a}\right)^2 - 1.8023\left(\dfrac{b}{a}\right)^3 + 0.9100\left(\dfrac{b}{a}\right)^4\right]$ at the midpoint of each longer side for $a \geq b$ |
