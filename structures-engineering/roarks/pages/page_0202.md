4. Intermediate externally created angular deformation

![Figure 8.1-4: Intermediate externally created angular deformation](figures/fig8_1_4.png)
<!-- Description: Beam diagram showing intermediate angular deformation at distance a, reactions and end quantities at A and B, with coordinates and slopes indicated. -->

Transverse shear $= V = R_A$

Bending moment $= M = M_A + R_A x$

Slope $= \theta = \theta_A + \dfrac{M_A x}{EI} + \dfrac{R_A x^2}{2EI} + \theta_o (x-a)^0$

Deflection $= y = y_A + \theta_A x + \dfrac{M_A x^2}{2EI} + \dfrac{R_A x^3}{6EI} + \theta_o (x-a)$
