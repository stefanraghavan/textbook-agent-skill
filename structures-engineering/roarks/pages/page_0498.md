# TABLE 11.2 Formulas for flat circular plates of constant thickness *(Continued)*

**19. Uniform load over a small eccentric circular area of radius $r_o$; edge fixed**

![Figure 19: Uniform load over a small eccentric circular area of radius ro; edge fixed](figures/fig19.png)
<!-- Description: Two diagrams showing an eccentrically loaded circular plate with fixed edge, dimensions o, p, ro, load W, and geometric distances r1, r2 from a point S. -->

At any point $s$,

$$
y=\frac{-W}{16\pi D}\left[\frac{p^2r_2^2}{a^2}-r_1^2\left(1+2\ln\frac{pr_2}{ar_1}\right)\right]
$$

(Note: As $p \to 0$, $pr_2 \to a^2$)

At the load point,

$$
y=\frac{-W}{16\pi D}\frac{(a^2-p^2)^2}{a^2}
$$

$$
M_r=\frac{W(1+\nu)}{16\pi}\left[4\ln\left(\frac{a-p}{r_o'}\right)+\left(\frac{a-p}{a-p}\right)\left(\frac{r_o'}{a-p}\right)^2\right]=M_{\max}
\quad \text{if } r_o' < 0.6(a-p)
$$

(Note: $r_o'$ defined in case 18)

At the near edge,

$$
M_r=\frac{-W}{8\pi}\left[2-\left(\frac{r_o'}{a-p}\right)^2\right]=M_{\max}
\quad \text{if } r_o > 0.6(a-p)
$$

[Formulas due to Michell (Ref. 2). See Ref. 60 for modified boundary conditions]

**20. Central couple on an annular plate with a simply supported outer edge (trunnion loading)**

![Figure 20: Central couple on an annular plate with a simply supported outer edge](figures/fig20.png)
<!-- Description: Diagram of an annular plate with inner radius b, outer radius a, applied central couple M, and rotation theta. -->

*(Note: For eccentric trunnions loaded with vertical loads, couples, and pressure on the plate, see Refs. 86 and 87)*

**20a. Trunnion simply supported to plate. For $\nu = 0.3$**

$$
\theta=\frac{\alpha M}{Et^3}
\qquad
\tau_{\max}=\tau_{rt}=\frac{\lambda M}{at^2}
\text{ at } r=b \text{ at } 90^\circ \text{ to the plane of } M
$$

$$
\sigma_{\max}=\sigma_t=\frac{\gamma M}{at^2}
\text{ at } r=b \text{ in the plane of } M
$$
