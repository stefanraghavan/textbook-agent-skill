# TABLE A.1 Properties of sections *(Continued)*

**27. Hollow semiellipse with constant wall thickness $t$.**  
The midthickness perimeter is an ellipse (shown dashed).  
$0.2 < a/b < 5$

![Figure 27: Hollow semiellipse with constant wall thickness](figures/fig27.png)
<!-- Description: A semielliptical hollow section with constant wall thickness t, showing dashed midthickness ellipse, coordinate axes x and y, dimensions a and b, centroid locations x_c, y_c1, and y_c2. -->

*Note:* There is a limit on the maximum wall thickness allowed in this case. Cusps will form in the perimeter at the ends of the major axis if this maximum is exceeded.

If $\dfrac{a}{b} \leq 1$, then $t_{\max} = \dfrac{2a^2}{b}$

If $\dfrac{a}{b} \geq 1$, then $t_{\max} = \dfrac{2b^2}{a}$

$$
A = \frac{\pi}{2} t(a+b)\left[1+K_1\left(\frac{a-b}{a+b}\right)^2\right]
$$

where

$$
K_1 = 0.2464 + 0.002222\left(\frac{a}{b}+\frac{b}{a}\right)
$$

$$
y_{c2}=\frac{2a}{\pi}K_2+\frac{t^2}{6\pi a}K_3
$$

where

$$
K_2 = 1 - 0.3314C + 0.0136C^2 + 0.1097C^3
$$

$$
K_3 = 1 + 0.9929C - 0.2287C^2 - 0.2193C^3
$$

Using

$$
C=\frac{a-b}{a+b}
$$

$$
y_{c1}=a+\frac{t}{2}-y_{c2}
$$

$$
x_c=b+\frac{t}{2}
$$

$$
I_x=\frac{\pi}{8}ta^2(a+3b)\left[1+K_4\left(\frac{a-b}{a+b}\right)^2\right]
+\frac{\pi}{32}t^3(3a+b)\left[1+K_5\left(\frac{a-b}{a+b}\right)^2\right]
$$

where

$$
K_4 = 0.1349 + 0.1279\frac{a}{b} - 0.01284\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^2
$$

$$
K_5 = 0.1349 + 0.1279\frac{b}{a} - 0.01284\left(\frac{b}{a}\right)^2
$$

$$
\bar{I}_x = I_x - Ay_{c2}^2
$$

For $\bar{I}_y$ use one-half the value for $\bar{I}_y$ in case 26.

Let $y_n$ be the vertical distance from the bottom to the plastic neutral axis.

$$
y_n=\left[C_1+\frac{C_2}{a/b}+\frac{C_3}{(a/b)^2}+\frac{C_4}{(a/b)^3}\right]a
$$

where if $0.25 < a/b \leq 1$, then

$$
C_1 = 0.5067 - 0.5588D + 1.3820D^2
$$

$$
C_2 = 0.3731 + 0.1938D - 1.4078D^2
$$

$$
C_3 = -0.1400 + 0.0179D + 0.4885D^2
$$

$$
C_4 = 0.0170 - 0.0079D - 0.0565D^2
$$

or if $1 \leq a/b < 4$, then

$$
C_1 = 0.4829 + 0.0725D - 0.1815D^2
$$

$$
C_2 = 0.1957 - 0.6608D + 1.4222D^2
$$

$$
C_3 = 0.0203 + 1.8999D - 3.4356D^2
$$

$$
C_4 = 0.0578 - 1.6666D + 2.6012D^2
$$

where $D=t/t_{\max}$ and where $0.2 < D \leq 1$

$$
Z_x=\left[C_5+\frac{C_6}{a/b}+\frac{C_7}{(a/b)^2}+\frac{C_8}{(a/b)^3}\right]4a^2t
$$

where if $0.25 < a/b \leq 1$, then

$$
C_5 = -0.0292 + 0.3749D^{1/2} + 0.0578D
$$

$$
C_6 = 0.3674 - 0.8531D^{1/2} + 0.3882D
$$

$$
C_7 = -0.1218 + 0.3563D^{1/2} - 0.1803D
$$

$$
C_8 = 0.0154 - 0.0448D^{1/2} + 0.0233D
$$

or if $1 \leq a/b < 4$, then

$$
C_5 = 0.2241 - 0.3922D^{1/2} + 0.2960D
$$

$$
C_6 = -0.6637 + 2.7357D^{1/2} - 2.0482D
$$

$$
C_7 = 1.5211 - 5.3864D^{1/2} + 3.9286D
$$

$$
C_8 = -0.8498 + 2.8763D^{1/2} - 1.8874D
$$

For $Z_y$ use one-half the value for $Z_y$ in case 26.
