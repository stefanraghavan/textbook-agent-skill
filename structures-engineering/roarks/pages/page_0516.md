## 9aaa. Uniform over $\frac{1}{3}$ of plate from fixed edge

![Figure 11.4: Uniform over 1/3 of plate from fixed edge](figures/fig11_4_9aaa.png)
<!-- Description: A schematic of a plate fixed at the bottom edge, with a uniformly distributed load applied over one-third of the plate from the fixed edge. The vertical axis is labeled z. -->

At $x = 0, z = 0$

$$
(\sigma_b)_{\max} = -\frac{\beta_1 q b^2}{t^2}
\qquad \text{and} \qquad
R = \gamma_1 q b
$$

At $x = 0, z = 0.2b$

$$
\sigma_b = \frac{\beta_2 q b^2}{t^2}
\qquad \text{and} \qquad
\sigma_a = \frac{\beta_3 q b^2}{t^2}
$$

At $x = 0, z = b$

$$
R = \gamma_2 q b
$$

At $x = \pm \frac{a}{2}, z = 0.2b$

$$
\sigma_a = -\frac{\beta_4 q b^2}{t^2}
\qquad \text{and} \qquad
R = \gamma_3 q b
$$
