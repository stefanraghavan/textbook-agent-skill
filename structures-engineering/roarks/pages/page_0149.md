For case 7f

$$
LF_H = W\left(C_{HH} - aC_{HM} + \frac{a^3}{6E_1I_1}\right)
$$

$$
= 1000\left[0.0003511 - 12(0.00001033) + \frac{12^3}{6(30 \cdot 10^6)(8)}\right]
$$

$$
= 1000(0.0003511 - 0.000124 + 0.0000012) = 0.2282\ \text{in}
$$

Similarly

$$
LF_V = 0.0442\ \text{in} \quad \text{and} \quad LF_M = 0.00632\ \text{rad}
$$

For case 7q

$$
LF_H = -(T - T_0)\gamma_3 l_3^3 = -50(0.0000065)(15) = -0.004875\ \text{in}
$$

$$
LF_V = 0.0065\ \text{in} \quad \text{and} \quad LF_M = 0\ \text{rad}
$$

For the combined loading

$$
LF_H = 0.2282 - 0.004875 = 0.2233\ \text{in}
$$

$$
LF_V = 0.0507\ \text{in}
$$

$$
LF_M = 0.00632\ \text{in}
$$

Now the left end force, moment, and displacement can be evaluated:

$$
V_A = \frac{LF_V C_{MM} - LF_M C_{VM}}{C_{VV}C_{MM} - C_{VM}^2}
= \frac{0.0507(0.359 \cdot 10^{-6}) - 0.00632(1.94 \cdot 10^{-6})}{(24.4 \cdot 10^{-6})(0.359 \cdot 10^{-6}) - (1.94 \cdot 10^{-6})^2}
$$

$$
= 1189\ \text{lb}
$$

$$
M_A = 11,179\ \text{lb-in}
$$

$$
\delta_{HA} = 0.0274\ \text{in}
$$
