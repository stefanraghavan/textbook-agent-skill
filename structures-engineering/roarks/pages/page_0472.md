**2j. Outer edge guided, inner edge fixed**

![Figure 2j](figures/fig2j.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge guided and inner edge fixed, showing inner radius $r_o$ and uniform load $q$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad \theta_b = 0,\quad \theta_a = 0,\quad Q_a = 0$

$$
M_{rb} = -\frac{qa^2}{C_5}\left[\frac{C_6}{2ab}(a^2-r_o^2)-L_{14}\right]
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{q}{2b}(a^2-r_o^2)
$$

$$
y_a = M_{rb}\frac{a^2}{D}C_2 + Q_b\frac{a^3}{D}C_3 - \frac{qa^4}{D}L_{11}
$$

$$
M_{ra} = M_{rb}C_8 + Q_baC_9 - qa^2L_{17}
$$

If $r_o = b$ (uniform load over entire plate),

| $b/a$ | 0.1 | 0.3 | 0.5 | 0.7 | 0.9 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $K_{y_a}$ | -0.0343 | -0.0123 | -0.0030 | -0.0004 |  |
| $K_{M_{rb}}$ | -0.7892 | -0.2978 | -0.1184 | -0.0359 | -0.00351 |
| $K_{M_{ra}}$ | 0.1146 | 0.0767 | 0.0407 | 0.0149 | 0.00167 |

**2k. Outer edge free, inner edge simply supported**

![Figure 2k](figures/fig2k.png)
<!-- Description: Schematic of a flat circular plate with outer edge free and inner edge simply supported, showing inner radius $r_o$ and uniform load $q$. -->

$y_b = 0,\quad M_{rb} = 0,\quad M_{ra} = 0,\quad Q_a = 0$

$$
\theta_b = -\frac{qa^3}{DC_7}\left[\frac{C_9}{2ab}(a^2-r_o^2)-L_{17}\right]
$$

$$
Q_b = \frac{q}{2b}(a^2-r_o^2)
$$

$$
y_a = \theta_baC_1 + Q_b\frac{a^3}{D}C_3 - \frac{qa^4}{D}L_{11}
$$

$$
\theta_a = \theta_bC_4 + Q_b\frac{a^2}{D}C_6 - \frac{qa^3}{D}L_{14}
$$
