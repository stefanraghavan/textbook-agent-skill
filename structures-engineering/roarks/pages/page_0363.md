## 2b. Right end fixed, left end simply supported

![Figure 2b](figures/fig2b.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with right end fixed, left end simply supported, subjected to moment $M_o$ and reaction $V_A$. Boundary conditions shown beneath the figure. -->

$M_A = 0 \qquad T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0$

$y_B = 0 \qquad \theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0$

$$
V_A = -\frac{M_o}{R}\frac{C_{a7}(1-\cos\phi)-C_{a4}\sin\phi+C_{a1}}{C_9(1-\cos\phi)-C_6\sin\phi+C_3}
$$

$$
\Theta_A = -\frac{M_oR}{EI}
\frac{(C_{a1}C_9-C_{a7}C_3)\sin\phi+(C_{a7}C_6-C_{a4}C_9)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_{a4}C_3-C_{a1}C_6)\cos\phi}
{C_9(1-\cos\phi)-C_6\sin\phi+C_3}
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{M_oR}{EI}
\frac{[(C_{a4}(C_9+C_3)-C_6(C_{a1}+C_{a7}))]\sin\phi+(C_{a7}C_3-C_{a1}C_9)\cos\phi}
{C_9(1-\cos\phi)-C_6\sin\phi+C_3}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
M_B = V_A R \sin\phi + M_o \cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B = V_A R(1-\cos\phi) + M_o \sin(\phi-\theta)
$$

## 2c. Right end fixed, left end supported and slope-guided

![Figure 2c](figures/fig2c.png)
<!-- Description: Curved beam with right end fixed, left end supported and slope-guided, subjected to moment $M_o$, with reactions $M_A$ and $V_A$. Boundary conditions shown beneath the figure. -->

$T_A = 0 \qquad y_A = 0 \qquad \Theta_A = 0$

$y_B = 0 \qquad \theta_B = 0 \qquad \psi_B = 0$

$$
V_A = -\frac{M_o}{R}
\frac{(C_{a7}C_4-C_{a4}C_7)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_{a4}C_1-C_{a1}C_4)\cos\phi+(C_{a1}C_7-C_{a7}C_1)\sin\phi}
{(C_4C_9-C_6C_7)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_1C_6-C_3C_4)\cos\phi+(C_3C_7-C_1C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
M_A = -M_o
\frac{(C_{a4}C_9-C_{a7}C_6)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_{a1}C_6-C_{a4}C_3)\cos\phi+(C_{a7}C_3-C_{a1}C_9)\sin\phi}
{(C_4C_9-C_6C_7)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_1C_6-C_3C_4)\cos\phi+(C_3C_7-C_1C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
\psi_A = -\frac{M_oR}{EI}
\frac{C_{a1}(C_4C_9-C_6C_7)+C_{a4}(C_3C_7-C_1C_9)+C_{a7}(C_1C_6-C_3C_4)}
{(C_4C_9-C_6C_7)(1-\cos\phi)+(C_1C_6-C_3C_4)\cos\phi+(C_3C_7-C_1C_9)\sin\phi}
$$

$$
V_B = V_A
$$

$$
M_B = V_A R \sin\phi + M_A \cos\phi + M_o \cos(\phi-\theta)
$$

$$
T_B = V_A R(1-\cos\phi) + M_A \sin\phi + M_o \sin(\phi-\theta)
$$
