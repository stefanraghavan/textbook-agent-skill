**5. Axial compressive load plus externally created concentrated lateral displacement**

![Figure 5](figures/fig5.png)
<!-- Description: General beam diagram showing axial compressive load P, reactions $R_A$, $R_B$, end moments $M_A$, $M_B$, end slope $\theta_A$, $\theta_B$, lateral displacement $\Delta_0$, coordinate axes X and Y, and distance a along beam length l. -->

Transverse shear
$$
V = R_A F_1 - M_A kF_2 - \theta_A P F_1 + \Delta_0 PkF_{a2}
$$

Bending moment
$$
M = M_A F_1 + \frac{R_A}{k}F_2 - \frac{\theta_A P}{k}F_2 - \Delta_0 P F_{a1}
$$

Slope
$$
\theta = \theta_A F_1 + \frac{M_A k}{P}F_2 + \frac{R_A}{P}F_3 - \Delta_0 kF_{a2}
$$

Deflection
$$
y = y_A + \frac{\theta_A}{k}F_2 + \frac{M_A}{P}F_3 + \frac{R_A}{kP}F_4 + \Delta_0 F_{a1}
$$
