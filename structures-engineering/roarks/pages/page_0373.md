4. Uniformly distributed lateral load

![Figure 9.4: Uniformly distributed lateral load on curved beam](figures/fig9_4.png)
<!-- Description: A curved beam segment with distributed load w, angular coordinates \phi, \theta, and x, showing reactions and end actions V_A, T_A, M_A at the left end; V_B, T_B, M_B at the right end; and internal actions M, T, V at a section. -->

Transverse shear $= V = V_A - wR\langle x - \theta \rangle^1$

Bending moment $= M = V_A R \sin x + M_A \cos x - T_A \sin x - wR^2[1 - \cos(x - \theta)]\langle x - \theta \rangle^0$

Twisting moment $= T = V_A R(1 - \cos x) + M_A \sin x + T_A \cos x - wR^2[x - \theta - \sin(x - \theta)]\langle x - \theta \rangle^0$

Vertical deflection $= y = y_A + \Theta_A R \sin x + \psi_A R(1 - \cos x) + \dfrac{M_A R^2}{EI}F_1 + \dfrac{T_A R^2}{EI}F_2 + \dfrac{V_A R^3}{EI}F_3 - \dfrac{wR^4}{EI}F_{a13}$

Bending slope $= \Theta = \Theta_A \cos x + \psi_A \sin x + \dfrac{M_A R}{EI}F_4 + \dfrac{T_A R}{EI}F_5 + \dfrac{V_A R^2}{EI}F_6 - \dfrac{wR^3}{EI}F_{a16}$

Roll slope $= \psi = \psi_A \cos x - \Theta_A \sin x + \dfrac{M_A R}{EI}F_7 + \dfrac{T_A R}{EI}F_8 + \dfrac{V_A R^2}{EI}F_9 - \dfrac{wR^3}{EI}F_{a19}$

For tabulated values: $V = K_V wR,\quad M = K_M wR^2,\quad T = K_T wR^2,\quad y = K_y \dfrac{wR^4}{EI},\quad \Theta = K_\Theta \dfrac{wR^3}{EI},\quad \psi = K_\psi \dfrac{wR^3}{EI}$
