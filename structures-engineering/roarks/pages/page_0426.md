# TABLE 10.3 Formulas for the elastic deformations of uniform thin-walled open members under torsional loading *(Continued)*

SEC. 10.7

---

**1g. Both ends fixed (no twist or warp)**

![Figure 10.3-1g: Both ends fixed (no twist or warp)](figures/fig10_3_1g.png)
<!-- Description: A thin-walled open member fixed at both ends, with a concentrated torque $T_0$ applied at a distance $a$ from the left end. -->

$$
\theta_A = 0,\qquad \theta'_A = 0
$$

$$
\theta''_A = \frac{T_0}{C_w E \beta}\,\frac{C_3C_{a4}-C_4C_{a3}}{C_2C_4-C_3^2}
$$

$$
T_A = -T_0\,\frac{C_2C_{a4}-C_3C_{a3}}{C_2C_4-C_3^2}
$$

$$
\theta_B = 0,\qquad \theta'_B = 0
$$

$$
\theta''_B = \theta''_A C_1 + \frac{T_A}{C_w E \beta}C_2 + \frac{T_0}{C_w E \beta}C_{a2}
$$

$$
\theta'''_B = \theta''_A \beta C_2 + \frac{T_A}{C_w E}C_1 + \frac{T_0}{C_w E}C_{a1}
$$

$$
T_B = -T_0 - T_A
$$

If $a=l/2$ (torque applied at midlength),

$$
T_A = T_B = -\frac{T_0}{2}
$$

$$
\theta_{\max} = \frac{T_0}{C_w E \beta^3}\left(\frac{\beta l}{4}-\tanh \frac{\beta l}{4}\right)
\qquad \text{at } x=\frac{l}{2}
$$

$$
\theta'_{\max} = \frac{T_0}{2C_w E \beta^2}\left[1-\frac{1}{\cosh(\beta l/4)}\right]
\qquad \text{at } x=\frac{l}{4}
$$

$$
\theta''_{\max} = \pm \frac{T_0}{2C_w E \beta}\tanh \frac{\beta l}{4}
\qquad \text{at } x=0,\; x=\frac{l}{2},\; \text{and } x=l,\; \text{respectively}
$$

$$
(-\theta''')_{\max} = -\frac{T_0}{2C_w E}
\qquad \text{at } x=0 \text{ and just left of } x=\frac{l}{2}
$$

$$
(+\theta''')_{\max} = \frac{T_0}{2C_w E}
\qquad \text{at } x=l \text{ and just right of } x=\frac{l}{2}
$$

**2. Uniformly distributed torque from $a$ to $l$**

![Figure 10.3-2: Uniformly distributed torque from a to l](figures/fig10_3_2.png)
<!-- Description: A thin-walled open member with end torque reaction $T_A$ at the left end, uniformly distributed torque $t_0$ applied from position $a$ to the right end $B$, with coordinates and twist indicated. -->

$$
\theta = \theta_A + \frac{\theta'_A}{\beta}F_2 + \frac{\theta''_A}{\beta^2}F_3 + \frac{T_A}{C_w E \beta^3}F_4 + \frac{t_0}{C_w E \beta^4}F_{a5}
$$

$$
\theta' = \theta'_A F_1 + \frac{\theta''_A}{\beta}F_2 + \frac{T_A}{C_w E \beta^2}F_3 + \frac{t_0}{C_w E \beta^3}F_{a4}
$$

$$
\theta'' = \theta''_A F_1 + \theta'_A \beta F_2 + \frac{T_A}{C_w E \beta}F_2 + \frac{t_0}{C_w E \beta^2}F_{a3}
$$

$$
\theta''' = \theta'_A \beta^2 F_1 + \theta''_A \beta F_2 + \frac{T_A}{C_w E}F_1 + \frac{t_0}{C_w E \beta}F_{a2}
$$

$$
T = T_A + t_0(x-a)
$$
