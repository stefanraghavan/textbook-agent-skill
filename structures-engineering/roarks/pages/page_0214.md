| 5n. | Concentrated angular displacement on the right vertical member | ![Figure 8.2-5n: Concentrated angular displacement on the right vertical member](figures/fig8_2_5n.png) <!-- Description: Frame with angular displacement $\theta_0$ applied on the right vertical member at a location marked by $a$. --> | $LF_H=\theta_0(l_1-a)$  <br> $LF_V=\theta_0(l_3)$  <br> $LF_M=\theta_0(1)$ |
| 5p. | Concentrated lateral displacement on the right vertical member | ![Figure 8.2-5p: Concentrated lateral displacement on the right vertical member](figures/fig8_2_5p.png) <!-- Description: Frame with lateral displacement $\Delta_0$ applied on the right vertical member at a location marked by $a$. --> | $LF_H=\Delta_0(-1)$  <br> *(Note: $\Delta_0$ could also be an increase in the length $l_3$)*  <br> $LF_V=0$  <br> $LF_M=0$ |
| 5q. | Uniform temperature rise:  <br> $T=$ uniform temperature  <br> $T_0=$ unloaded temperature | ![Figure 8.2-5q: Uniform temperature rise](figures/fig8_2_5q.png) <!-- Description: Frame subjected to a uniform temperature rise. --> | $LF_H=(T-T_0)(-\gamma_3 l_3)$  <br> $LF_V=(T-T_0)(\gamma_1 l_1-\gamma_2 l_2)$  <br> $LF_M=0$  <br> $\gamma=$ temperature coefficient of expansion (inches/inch/degree) |
| 5r. | Uniform temperature differential from outside to inside; average temperature is $T_0$ | ![Figure 8.2-5r: Uniform temperature differential from outside to inside](figures/fig8_2_5r.png) <!-- Description: Frame with temperature differential, labeled $T_1$ on the outside and $T_2$ on the inside. --> | $LF_H=(T_1-T_2)\left[\dfrac{l_1^2\gamma_1}{2t_1}+\dfrac{l_2^2\gamma_2}{2t_2}(2l_1-l_2)+\dfrac{l_1l_3\gamma_3}{t_3}\right]$  <br> $LF_V=(T_1-T_2)\left(\dfrac{l_2l_3\gamma_2}{t_2}+\dfrac{l_3^2\gamma_3}{2t_3}\right)$  <br> $LF_M=(T_1-T_2)\left(\dfrac{l_1\gamma_1}{t_1}+\dfrac{l_2\gamma_2}{t_2}+\dfrac{l_3\gamma_3}{t_3}\right)$  <br> $t_1$, $t_2$, and $t_3$ are beam thicknesses from inside to outside |
| 6. | Left end pinned, right end fixed | ![Figure 8.2-6: Left end pinned, right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_6.png) <!-- Description: Portal frame with left end pinned at A and right end fixed at B, showing reactions $H_A$, $V_A$, $H_B$, $V_B$, and moment $M_B$. --> | Since $\delta_{HA}=0$, $\delta_{VA}=0$ and $M_A=0$,  <br><br> $H_A=\dfrac{LF_H\,C_{VV}-LF_V\,C_{HV}}{C_{HH}C_{VV}-(C_{HV})^2}$  <br> $V_A=\dfrac{LF_V\,C_{HH}-LF_H\,C_{HV}}{C_{HH}C_{VV}-(C_{HV})^2}$  <br> $\psi_A=C_{MH}H_A+C_{MV}V_A-LF_M$  <br><br> Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r |
| 7. | Left end guided horizontally, right end fixed | ![Figure 8.2-7: Left end guided horizontally, right end fixed](figures/fig8_2_7.png) <!-- Description: Portal frame with left end guided horizontally at A and right end fixed at B, showing reactions $V_A$, $M_A$, $H_B$, $V_B$, and moment $M_B$. --> | Since $\delta_{VA}=0$, $\psi_A=0$ and $H_A=0$,  <br><br> $V_A=\dfrac{LF_V\,C_{MM}-LF_M\,C_{VM}}{C_{VV}C_{MM}-(C_{VM})^2}$  <br> $M_A=\dfrac{LF_M\,C_{VV}-LF_V\,C_{VM}}{C_{VV}C_{MM}-(C_{VM})^2}$  <br> $\delta_{HA}=C_{HV}V_A+C_{HM}M_A-LF_H$  <br><br> Use the loading terms for cases 5a to 5r |
