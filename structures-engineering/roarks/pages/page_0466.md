## 1e. Outer edge fixed, inner edge free

![Figure 1e](figures/fig1e.png)
<!-- Description: Diagram of a flat circular plate with outer edge fixed, inner edge free, showing inner radius $r_o$ and load $w$. -->

$$
M_{rb}=0,\quad Q_b=0,\quad y_a=0,\quad \theta_a=0
$$

$$
y_b=\frac{-wa^3}{D}\left(\frac{C_1L_6}{C_4}-L_3\right)
$$

$$
\theta_b=\frac{wa^2}{DC_4}L_6
$$

$$
M_{ra}=-wa\left(L_9-\frac{C_7L_6}{C_4}\right)
$$

$$
Q_a=\frac{-wr_o}{a}
$$

If $r_o=b$ (load at inner edge),

|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $K_{y_b}$ | -0.0143 | -0.0330 | -0.0233 | -0.0071 | -0.0003 |
| $K_{\theta_b}$ | 0.0254 | 0.0825 | 0.0776 | 0.0373 | 0.0048 |
| $K_{M_{ra}}$ | -0.0528 | -0.1687 | -0.2379 | -0.2124 | -0.0911 |
| $K_{M_{tb}}$ | 0.2307 | 0.2503 | 0.1412 | 0.0484 | 0.0048 |

(Note: $|M_{ra}| > |M_{tb}|$ if $b/a > 0.385$)

## 1g. Outer edge fixed, inner edge simply supported

![Figure 1g](figures/fig1g.png)
<!-- Description: Diagram of a flat circular plate with outer edge fixed, inner edge simply supported, showing inner radius $r_o$ and load $w$. -->

$$
y_b=0,\quad M_{rb}=0,\quad y_a=0,\quad \theta_a=0
$$

$$
\theta_b=\frac{-wa^2}{D}\,\frac{C_3L_6-C_6L_3}{C_1C_6-C_3C_4}
$$

$$
Q_b=w\,\frac{C_1L_6-C_4L_3}{C_1C_6-C_3C_4}
$$

$$
M_{ra}=\theta_b\frac{D}{a}C_7+Q_baC_9-waL_9
$$

$$
Q_a=Q_b\frac{b}{a}-\frac{wr_o}{a}
$$
