# TABLE 13.4 Formulas for discontinuity stresses and deformations at the junctions of shells and plates *(Continued)*

**8d. Rotation around the axis of symmetry at $\omega$ rad/s**

![Figure 13.4: Rotation around the axis of symmetry at ω rad/s](figures/fig13_4.png)
<!-- Description: Sketch of two joined spherical shells rotating about the horizontal axis of symmetry, with angular velocity ω indicated on the right. -->

*Note: $\delta =$ mass/unit volume*

$$
LT_{A1}=-\frac{R_1^2\sin^3\phi_1}{t_1^2}
$$

$$
LT_{A2}=-\frac{\delta_2R_2^3E_1\sin^3\phi_2}{\delta_1R_1E_2t_1^2}
$$

$$
LT_{AC}=0
$$

$$
LT_{B1}=-\frac{R_1\sin\phi_1\cos\phi_1(3+\nu_1)}{t_1}
$$

$$
LT_{B2}=-\frac{\delta_2R_2^2E_1(3+\nu_2)\sin\phi_2\cos\phi_2}{\delta_1R_1t_1E_2}
$$

$$
LT_{BC}=0
$$

At the junction of the two spheres,

$$
V_1=\delta_1\omega^2R_1t_1^2K_{V1}
$$

$$
M_1=\delta_1\omega^2R_1t_1^3K_{M1}
$$

$$
N_1=-V_1\cos\phi_1
$$

$$
\Delta R_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2R_1^2t_1^2}{E_1}\left(LT_{A1}-K_{V1}C_{AA1}-K_{M1}C_{AB1}\right)
$$

$$
\psi_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2R_1t_1}{E_1}\left(-LT_{B1}+K_{V1}C_{AB1}+K_{M1}C_{BB1}\right)
$$

For $E_1=E_2$, $\nu_1=\nu_2=0.3$, $t_1=t_2$, $R_1\sin\phi_1=R_2\sin\phi_2$, $\delta_1=\delta_2$, and for $R/t>5$.

$$
\Delta R_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2R_1^3}{E_1}K_{\Delta RA}, \qquad
\psi_A=\frac{\delta_1\omega^2R_1^2}{E_1}K_{\psi A}, \qquad
\sigma_2=\delta_1\omega^2R_1^2K_{\sigma 2}
$$
