<!-- Description: Circular ring with top point A, bottom point C, angular markings $\theta$, distributed radial pressure over the lower arc, downward load indication $w$, and dimension $2wR(1+c)/(\pi-\theta)$. -->

The radial pressure $w_x$ varies linearly with $x$ from 0 at $x=\theta$ to $w$ at $x=\pi$.

$$LT_M=\frac{-wR^2}{\pi-\theta}(x-\theta-zc+us)(x-\theta)^0$$

$$LT_N=\frac{-wR}{\pi-\theta}(x-\theta-zc+us)(x-\theta)^0$$

$$LT_V=\frac{-wR}{\pi-\theta}(1-uc-zs)(x-\theta)^0$$

$$M_C=\frac{-wR^2}{\pi(\pi-\theta)}\left\{\pi(\pi-\theta)-2-2c-s\theta+k_2\left[1+c-\frac{(\pi-\theta)^2}{2}\right]\right\}$$

$$N_A=\frac{-wR}{\pi(\pi-\theta)}[2+2c-s(\pi-\theta)]$$

$$V_A=0$$

$$\Delta D_H=
\begin{cases}
\dfrac{-wR^4}{EI(\pi-\theta)}\left\{k_1\left(1-\dfrac{s\theta}{2}\right)+k_2(\pi-2\theta-2c)+k_2^2\dfrac{2+2c-(\pi-\theta)^2}{\pi}\right\} & \text{for } \theta\leqq \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\
\dfrac{-wR^4}{EI(\pi-\theta)}\left\{k_1\left[1+c-\dfrac{s(\pi-\theta)}{2}\right]+k_2^2\dfrac{2+2c-(\pi-\theta)^2}{\pi}\right\} & \text{for } \theta\geqq \dfrac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}$$

$$\Delta D_V=\frac{wR^4}{EI(\pi-\theta)}\left\{k_1\frac{s+c(\pi-\theta)}{2}-k_2(\pi-\theta-s)-k_2^2\frac{2+2c-(\pi-\theta)^2}{\pi}\right\}$$

$$\Delta L=
\begin{cases}
\dfrac{wR^4}{2EI\pi(\pi-\theta)}\left\{k_1\left(\dfrac{\pi^2c}{2}-2c+2\pi-2-\theta s\right)-k_2\pi\left[2(\pi-\theta)-1+c-\dfrac{\pi^2}{4}+\dfrac{\theta^2}{2}\right]-k_2^2[2+2c-\pi-\theta)^2\right\} & \text{for } \theta\leqq \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\
\dfrac{wR^4}{2EI\pi(\pi-\theta)}\left\{k_1[\pi c(\pi-\theta)+2\pi s-2c-2-\theta s]-k_2\pi\left[2(\pi-\theta)+1+c-2s-\dfrac{(\pi-\theta)^2}{2}\right]-k_2^2[2+2c-(\pi-\theta)^2]\right\} & \text{for } \theta\geqq \dfrac{\pi}{2}
\end{cases}$$
