236 FLOW TO RUN FULL

The continuity equation can be written as:

$$
dQ = udA,
$$

(23.4)

where $dA = 2xdy$ and $x = \sqrt{R^2 - y^2}$. Substitution gives:

$$
dQ = 2\sqrt{2g\left(\frac{\rho_{heavy}-\rho_{light}}{\rho_{heavy}}\right)}(H-y)\sqrt{R^2-y^2}\,dy.
$$

The integral form in the case of open flow $(H < R)$ is:

$$
Q = 2\sqrt{2g\left(\frac{\rho_{heavy}-\rho_{light}}{\rho_{heavy}}\right)} \int_{-R}^{H} \sqrt{(H-y)(R^2-y^2)}\,dy.
$$

(23.5)

The Froude number can be expressed as:

$$
N_{Fr} = \frac{Q}{\pi\sqrt{2g}R^{5/2}}.
$$

(23.6)

Substitution of Equation 23.5 into Equation 23.6 gives:

$$
N_{Fr} = \frac{2\sqrt{\frac{\rho_{heavy}-\rho_{light}}{\rho_{heavy}}}}{\pi R^{5/2}} \int_{-R}^{H} \sqrt{(H-y)(R^2-y^2)}\,dy.
$$

(23.7)
