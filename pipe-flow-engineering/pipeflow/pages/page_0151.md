## 13.3 ROUND-EDGED ORIFICE

Rounding of the leading edge of an orifice can considerably diminish or eliminate the vena contracta and thus substantially reduce the head loss. For a circular edge, the rounding radius $r$ is simply the radius of the quarter circle. In cases where the amount of rounding is limited by the radial distance available between the pipe wall and the orifice face, rounding may take the form of an ellipse or other curved shape.
